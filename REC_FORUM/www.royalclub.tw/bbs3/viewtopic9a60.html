Session 1
Bill and Melinda Gates are ending their marriage
https://edition.cnn.com/2021/05/03/tech ... index.html
Question
1. What is the real purpose of marriage? What is your opinion about marriage?
2. Does marriage need to give up oneself, shouldn’t have privacy or personal desires? If not, what should be done?
3. The divorce rate in Taiwan has always been quite high. Among them, the proportion of divorces only after middle age is increasing. Do you know why?
4. What do you think are the main conditions for maintaining a good marriage relationship? Money, education, family background, personality, living habits or values, etc. Which do you think are more important? why?
5. What is the idea marriage in your mind? Do you think it is possible and how do you achieve it?

Session 2
Taiwan’s total of COVID cases near 1500
https://www.taiwannews.com.tw/en/news/4203958
1. When COVID-19 broke out in other countries and even closed the city, Taiwan may not be really understanded their fear in relatively safe Taiwan. Now it is Taiwan turn, if Taiwan also implements the lockdown, how would you feel?
2. If our government order a two-week lockdown to prevent the epidemic in 24 hours, what measures will you take? Do you buy food or other items in bulk?
3. A month ago, you might not want to be vaccinated. If vaccinations were the best preventive measures and available, would you reconsider receiving vaccinations? Why?
4. So far, Taiwan has entered the third level of epidemic prevention measures. Has this measure caused any inconvenience and impact on you?
5. There is a shortage of nurses in Taipei. If you are a retired nurse, are you willing to contribute yourself? Why?
