How to Improve Your Social Life

http://www.wikihow.com/Improve-Your-Social-Life

Discussion:
Q1 What is your Hobbies (what would you do together?) Demeanor (serious and academic vs. humorous goof-balls, or a mix of both) Interests (similar tastes in books, movies, music, sports, etc.)Conversation habits (sit, relax and talk vs. do a lot of activities) Energy levels (raging at a bar vs. chilling at a quiet cafe)?
Q2 Have you ever invite your friends for dinner? Share one invitation experience which you were impressed by.
Q3 How frequency did you usually contact friends? Share the way how you maintain relationship.
Q4 In the method 2-2” Ask your current friends to bring their friends to events”, how do you think about this method? Does it work?P.S.

Session 2: 5 Ways to Make Smarter Life Decisions
https://www.lifehack.org/articles/produ ... sions.html

1. Identify Your Goals
2. Analyze the Pros and Cons
3. Learn from Your Mistakes
4. Learn from Other People’s Mistakes
5. Follow Your Intuition

Discussion Questions:
1. Do you agree with the “5 Ways to Make Smarter Life Decisions” in the article? Any other tips?
2. Among the 5 tips, which one or ones do you think are more important when making smarter life decisions?
3. What’re the smarter or worse life decisions you have made in your life? Why?
4. Looking back, was there any life decision that if you hadn’t made it that way you would lead a different life or you would be a different person now?
