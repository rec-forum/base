Session I

What Role Should Work Play in Retirement?

https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/blog ... retirement

Q1. In your opinion, what is a good age to retire? How much money do most people need to retire at that age?

Q2. How do you fill out a retired day? Would you plan to work (including part-time or volunteer work) in retirement? Why or why not?

Q3. What do you think is most important in retirement life? What advice would you give to those who have not prepared for their retirement?


Session II
How to Liven Up Leisure Time and Beat Boredom

https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/blog ... at-boredom

Q1. Do you think you have enough leisure time? Are you occupied with obligations and honey-do lists? What are they?

Q2. Do you know any people who feel bored in their leisure time or have “leisure time blues”? What lifestyles or habits do they usually have? What is your advice to break the boredom habits?

Q3. What is on your bucket list? What have you completed and what would you still like to achieve?
