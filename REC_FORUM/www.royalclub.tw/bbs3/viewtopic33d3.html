Dear REC members:

In case government announces a lockdown in Taipei & New Taipei Cities and we want to have a nonstop meeting, we are preparing our online meeting.

Please join us to discuss this COVID-19 pandemic at 7:30pm on Thursday 4/2.

Please download "Zoom" app to your computer, notebook, cellular phone, or tablet.
Then open the app, press the button "加入會議" (No need to 登入), then key in my meeting ID:3047940885, then you're in.


Regarding this COVID-19 outbreak, let's think of the following questions and express your perspective.

1. How do you think Chinese government put a lockdown in Wuhan? good or bad? effective or not?
2. Why is situation so dire in Europe?
3. What causes US become #1 country of confirmed infected cases?
4. In your company, are you able to telecommute during pandemic period
5. How is your company meeting or meeting with clients, do you use video conference call?
6. What can we learn from this pandemic until now?

Talk to you then!
