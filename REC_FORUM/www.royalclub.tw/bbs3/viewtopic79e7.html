05/11 (Thu) What are the benefits of dancing?/ 17 Proven Tips to Sleep Better at Night (Host: Joyce)


We will hold an on-site meeting only.
摩斯漢堡明曜店(每人低消 :60元)
台北市仁愛路4段151巷37號地下室.
MRT忠孝敦化站3號出口 , 明曜百貨後方第二個巷子口

Royal 台北 ( Thursday )
**** NEW*** Meeting time agenda:7:00-9:00 PM
7:00-7:30 Chatting time with your favorite drink, and introduction of the meeting by the host
7:30-8:00 discussion of the first topic
8:00-8:05 Take a group photo
8:05-8:30 discussion of the second topic
8:30-9:00 Individual Presentation (each person will have two minutes to give a presentation on any topic of that day.
)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Session 1, What are the benefits of dancing?
https://www.openaccessgovernment.org/be ... %20anxiety.

Questions:
1.Do you dance? Why most men won’t dance? Do you think the majority of men have very hard rhythmic timing and they don’t know how to move their bodies?
2.What are the pros and cons of dancing?
3.Why does your other half hate seeing you social dance with others? How to social dance without losing your partner?
4.Do you think most of the time, women are faster natural dancers than men?
5.What are the pros and cons of being a dancer?


Session 2, 17 Proven Tips to Sleep Better at Night!
https://www.healthline.com/nutrition/17 ... eep-better

Questions:
1.Do you often find yourself unable to get to sleep or regularly waking up night at night? Are you tired of tossing and turning at night?
2.Does personality influence sleep patterns?
3.How to sleep better?
4.Do sleep gadgets and apps help you fall asleep? How can technology help you fall asleep faster?
5. Diet, exercise, and sleep are three pillars of a healthy life. How to improve these lifestyle factors to help people lead longer lives?
