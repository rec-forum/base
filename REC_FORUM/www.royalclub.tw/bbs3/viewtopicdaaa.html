Place: 丹堤咖啡, 台北市延平南路107號2樓 (MRT 西門站4號出口)
Agenda:
07:00-7:05 pm Opening Remarks
07:05-7:40 pm Session 1 Group Discussion
07:40-8:00 pm Presentation (At least two members of each table are invited, 2 mins / each member)
8:00-8:05 pm Break
8:05-8:40 pm Session 2 discussion
8:40-9:00 pm Presentation

Session I: Happier Life or Live Longer?

A philosopher asks: Is a longer life a happier one?
https://edition.cnn.com/2018/07/23/heal ... hy-partner

Being happier will help you live longer, so learn how to be happier
https://edition.cnn.com/2019/09/30/heal ... r-wellness

Questions:
1. Do you think there is a ceiling on how long human beings can live? Do you agree that there is a "mortality plateau" as mentioned in the first article?
2. If possible, to what age do you wish to live? Why?
3. Do your goals and desires change at different stages of your life? What life goals do you think you would have if you can live very long?
4. If you could wish for just one thing, would it be happiness or a long life?
5. How do you obtain happiness? In your opinion, what creates a sustained feeling of happiness?

Session II: Acquired Taste

An acquired taste? McDonald's China offers burger featuring Spam and crushed Oreos
https://edition.cnn.com/travel/article/ ... index.html

Questions:
1. Do you think taste is innate or acquired? If acquired, can you acquire a taste for anything?
2. Do you love or hate the following foods: black coffee, blue cheese, sushi, dark chocolate, oysters, pickles, kimchi, liver, and cilantro? Do you think they are simply good, truly bad, or actually an acquired taste?
3. What kind of new foods have you tried recently? What is the most bizarre food you have ever tried? What is a food that you hated as a child but now love to eat?
4. Do you love to try creative or exotic foods (say, Domino's bubble milk tea pizza), or do you have a distinctive fixation on food and just want your food to “taste good”? What type of food do you like most?
5. How would you describe Taiwanese food culture?
