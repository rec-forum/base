Discussions ~
1)Have you noticed an increase in food prices recently? If so, which foods have you noticed have gone up in price the most?
2)What do you think are the main factors contributing to rising food prices?
3)Do you think there are differences in the reasons why food prices are increasing in developed versus developing countries? If so, what are they?
4)How do you think rising food prices are affecting different populations, such as low-income families, farmers, or small business owners?
5)Are there any solutions or strategies you can think of that could help mitigate the effects of rising food prices, either at an individual or societal level?

2nd article
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hK6mdoxXrIc
How Perfectionism Makes Us Ill

Discussions~
1)The article argues that perfectionism can lead to negative physical and mental health outcomes. Have you ever experienced this yourself, or observed it in someone else?

2)The article suggests that perfectionism can be caused by a variety of factors, including social pressures, fear of failure, and past experiences. What do you think are some other factors that might contribute to perfectionism?

3)The article discusses how perfectionism can lead to anxiety, depression, and other mental health issues. How do you think these negative outcomes can be mitigated or addressed?

4)The article suggests that a growth mindset, in which people focus on progress rather than perfection, can be a more positive approach to achieving goals. How do you think this mindset could be cultivated in individuals who struggle with perfectionism?

5)The article also suggests that mindfulness and self-compassion can be helpful in reducing the negative effects of perfectionism. Have you ever practiced mindfulness or self-compassion techniques? If so, what benefits have you noticed?
