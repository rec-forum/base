Session I: The other woman

Discussion:
1. Husband has extramarital affair with a mistress. It is mistress's responsibility to compensate the marriage? Or husband's and mistress's? Or
others'?
2. How do you feel "There is no family that can’t be wrecked "?
3. South Korea has decriminalized adultery for 3 years.
Source: http://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-31635747
Should Taiwan follow suit? Why or why not?
4. How to keep your love /marriage sweet / stable forever?
5. In 2017 the divorce rate of Taiwan has increased to 2.3%, ranking No 2 in Asia or No 4 in the world. Do you think cohabitation or marriage is
better for Taiwanese?
