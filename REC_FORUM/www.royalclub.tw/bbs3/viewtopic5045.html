7/15 (Thu.) Silver linings of Covid (host: Rock) (under construction)

Hi, guys, long time no see. My pleasure to host in Royal again. I'd like to invite you guys to talk about Covid, but only on the positive side. There is too much bad news already. This time, let's stick to the good.

The lockdown is coming to the end, and we can hopefully live like what we did before. Covid is very bad, but even the worst storm has its silver lining. What good things happened in the world thanks to this pandemic? What things in your life that are actually good because of Covid?

https://www.predictiveindex.com/blog/co ... r-linings/
https://www.quora.com/What-are-some-sil ... 9-outbreak

Questions
Session One
1. Many people work from home because of Covid; some people actually enjoy it. What are the good and fun things of WFH?
2. Students learn from home in Covid lockdown. What's good about LFH? What are the things of LFH that are better than real school?
3. What positive things do you have because of Covid 19?

Session Two
4. What positive things happened in the world thanks to Covid?
5. What are the worst things happened in Covid times? How can people handle them and find a silver linings of them?
6. What lessons can human beings learn from Covid?
