Questions:

1. What is your memory about Glove Puppetry and Taiwanese Opera? Have you ever been a fan to it?
2. Say something about your favorite character, troupe or show about Taiwanese Glove Puppetry and Taiwanese Opera. What is the amazing part to you?
3. Have you ever watched a show performed on stage instead of on TV? How do you feel about the on-stage performance?
4. Because of multiple alternative recreational activities, Glove Puppetry and Taiwanese Opera now become entertainment for only a small group of people., even considered old-fashioned. What do you think is still worthy to preserve for educational purpose?

Session 2: Taliban are back - what next for Afghanistan?
https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-49192495

Questions:

1. How much do you know about the terrorist attack by al-Qaeda in 2001 in NY? What were you doing when you heard about this sensational breaking news?
2. How much do you know about Afghanistan? Where is this country and why is it called “imperial cemetery” （帝國墳場）? Why were there always-endless wars and battles?
3. How much do you know about Sharia Law （伊斯蘭律法）? It looks barbaric and cruel and against human right. What can we do about countries like Afghanistan, Myanmar military government and North Korea?
4. Did USA betray and sell Afghanistan people? After 20 years military occupation and enforcement of western democratic values over there.
5. USA is still so far the world leader and even world cop of the world. Do you welcome a new world order in which USA can no longer dominate?
