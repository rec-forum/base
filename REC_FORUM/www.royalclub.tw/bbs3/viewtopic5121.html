Session I:
Will Aliens come to destroy the Earth?
https://www.space.com/29999-stephen-haw ... anger.html

Questions

1. Do you believe there are smarter, more advanced extraterrestrial being in the universe? Do you think it’s a good idea to discover them? Do you think it’s wise to keep on sending signals outwards to detect possible passers-by?
2. Someday, in case extraterrestrial aliens visited the Earth, do you tend to believe they come in peace to explore and do some study? Or they probably come to loot or to colonize?
3. There have been always many famous alien-related science fiction film, i.e. Close Encounters of the Third Kind (第三類接觸1977), ET(外星人1982), The X-Files (Drama, 1993), War of the Worlds(世界大戰Tom Cruise, 2005), ID4(星際終結者Will Smith, 1996). Which one of these or else do you prefer? Why?
4. Do you believe the Roswell UFO incident happened in Roswell, New Mexico, USA, 1947/7/4? In a storm night an UFO crashed to the ground and days after that a couple of “small man” were found dead inside a wreckage of a saucer-like object. Just 6 hours after the local press reported it on 7/9, the army said it’s but a meteorological balloon. What do you believe?


Session II:
How to spot false posts?
https://www.indiatoday.in/fact-check/st ... 2022-02-27
https://www.bbc.com/news/blogs-trending-60654288

Questions

1. Have you ever received post on social media but proved to be false afterwards? What was it, for instance?
2. Why are there always people who like to fabricate news and to spread it over?
3. Would you normally believe those posts on social media and share it forward? Or would you doubt and verify (ex. google) it for reality?
4. Do you know Taiwan Fact Check Center (台灣事實查核中心)? Do you know https://tineye.com? They help you verify a post is true or false. Will you check?
