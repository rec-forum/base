Session I: The Science of Depression

https://tw.voicetube.com/videos/22680


Questions:
1.Have you ever confused of your life or future? What are your confusions? Have your ever talked to others about it? Do you think it’s helpful if you talk to other people about your struggles?

2.Do you have seasonal affective disorder(季節性情緒失調)? Which season makes you feel sad or happy? Why?

3.Do you or your friends who ever have depression and go to see the doctor? Did you or they feel better after see the doctor?

4.Do you think mental disease is more serious than the physical disease? Why or why not?

5.How do you release your stress, depression, or difficulties? Can you share your ways with us?

https://tw.voicetube.com/videos/45291

4.What is the unforgettable thing that you did in your student life? Please share with us.

5.If you can back to your student life, what thing that you want to do it again?
