Session I
How Parents Get In The Way of Career Plans
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=82aa881dpr0


Discussions:
1. How did you choose your career? By your own, by family or other reasons?
2. How did or will you guide your children for their career?
3. What's the most important for you to choose a job?
4. Do you ever think about to switch to a totally different career ?
If you already did, share your experience. (or share from your friends)

Session II
How To Be A Good Teacher
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-FkvBA3U5lg


Discussions:
1. We all have chance to be a teacher. What is your tips?
2. Did you have any good or bad experience from your school teacher?
3. Do you prefer eastern or western teaching style?
4. Does the school district really matter?
