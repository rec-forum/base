Session 1
Sitting is the new smoking: ‘Truly a silent killer’
https://www.siouxfalls.business/sitting ... nt-killer/

Question
1. Would you take the research report seriously and share with
your family or friends?
2. How do you deal with sedentary problems if you need to sit all
day at the office?
3. Do you think you are an active person or an inactive person?
Are active people necessarily healthier than inactive people?
Why?
4. Do you know what’s the silent killer of mental health? Why?
5. Do you know what’s the silent killer of earth? Why?

Session 2
Are you ready for a 3D-printed house? They're cheaper, stronger and long-lasting
house? They’re cheaper, stronger and long-lasting, developers say

https://www.seattletimes.com/business/a ... opers-say/

Question
1. We know 3D-printed house are cheaper, stronger and long- lasting, what are other advantages of this kind of house?
2. If you want to buy a house, would you like to buy 3D-printed or traditional house? Why?
3. Do you think 3D- printed house are the future of house? Why?
4. Since 3D printer can print a lot of items, if you have a printer,
what items will you print? Why?
5. Every year all kinds of new technology products come out, have
you ever tried some new products before? If so, please share with us.
