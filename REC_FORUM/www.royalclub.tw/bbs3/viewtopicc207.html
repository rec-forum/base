Hi royal member, I didn’t print out the content of these two articles because I hope participants can practice English listening by watching/listening the attached youtube video website. There are many items you can adjust in the settings including languish, speaking speed, subtitle etc. to make you better comprehend it and be able to repeat listening until you feel comfortable .Let’s join this English learning journey together and express your opinion during Thursday gathering. Thanks.

Session 1.The Science of Laziness

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gd7wAithl7I

Question
1. Do you think laziness is human nature? Do you have couch potato gene?
2. If not, what cause you to laziness? Please come up with some reasons to let you be lazy?
3. What can be against laziness?
4. Please list some lazy examples that caused you trouble in your memory, how did you fight this laziness in the end? Or you give up to let laziness dominate you
5. Do you agree “Knowledge is power” in the article mentioned? If yes, make some examples to prove it.

Session 2 Talent is Trainable?

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2ZiRZrYo5tA

Question
1. Do you agree with the opinion of this video that talent is trainable? Or some talent is impossible to be trained, like what?
2. What kind of talent do you want to own if possible?
3. If you agree with point 1, what effort do you want to make to achieve your goal?
4. If you don’t agree with point 1, can you share your experience with members about “embarrassing story”
5. Share some difficult training you had before, and what happen after this training?
