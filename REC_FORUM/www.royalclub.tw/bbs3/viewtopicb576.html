Session One: Why the way we talk to children really matters ?

Please click the following link to read the article.
http://www.bbc.com/future/story/20191001-the-word-gap-that-affects-how-your-babys-brain-grows

Discussion:
Q1. Do you agree that the quality of the conversation to children is important?
Q2. Is there a word gap in children between lower-income and higher-income families?
Q3. Can you share experience in conversation when talking to a newborn?
Q4. How do you communicate effectively with kids?

Session Two: Why do people join cults ?

Please click the following link to watch the video.
https://www.ted.com/talks/janja_lalich_why_do_people_join_cults#t-367292

Discussion:
Q1. What is your opinion on religious cults? What do you consider a cult?
Q2. Why do higher education people join cults?
Q3. Why do people follow dictators?
Q4. What would you do if you found out a coworker who you like is a current member of an abusive religious cult?
