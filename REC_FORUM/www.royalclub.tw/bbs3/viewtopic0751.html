Session 1
8 Habits of Super-Productive People Who Work From Home - How to keep yourself accountable, collaborative, and productive — even when working from home
https://medium.com/mind-cafe/8-habits-o ... 770f3d8333

Q1: Do you prefer working from home or in an office? What are the pros and cons of working from home?
Q2. In your opinion, is working from home less productive? How do you stay organized when you work at home?
Q3. If you are a supervisor, how do you manage your remote workers? How do you measure their productivity?
Q4. If you can choose a weekday to work from home, which day is the best day to telecommute? What are your reasons for that?

Session 2
The 7 Habits of Highly Ineffective People
https://medium.com/mind-cafe/the-7-habi ... 4b0ff317be
7 Habits of Super Organised People: Being organised isn’t just a habit; it’s a way of life.
https://medium.com/mind-cafe/7-habits-o ... ee6bd974aa

Q1. From your observations, what do organized people have in common? What makes organized people organized?
Q2. In your opinions, what are ineffective people have in common? What makes ineffective people ineffective?
Q3. Is being organized important? Are you an organized person? If so, what does being organized say about you?
Q4. Do you agree with the authors’ opinions on the above articles? What is your advice to become more efficient and orderly at work, at home and in life, or to avoid inefficiency?
