Session I: 4 Best Things to Do in your Free Time

Please watch the video, and join the meeting to share you ideas and practise English.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d9W2GO1LhP4

According to the video, the four best things to do when we are bored are:
1. Exercise
2. Reading
3. Meditation
4. Hobby/Passion

Questions:
1. If you watched the videos, please summarize what you have learned and share it with other table members. What are the four best things to do when you are bored? Which one is the most helpful to you?
2. How much free time do you usually have on a working day and on a holiday, respectively? How do you spend it? Do you think you live a busy life or a lazy one? Do or will you do any things mentioned in the video?
3. Besides the four things mentioned in the video, what are other things we can do to enrich our lives?

Session II: How to persuade people?

Please watch the video, and join the meeting to share you idears and practise English.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xmkfg_dcHFI

According to the video, the six tips/secrets to persuade others are:
1. Reciprocity
2. Scarcity
3. Authority
4. Consistency
5. Linking
6. Consensus

Questions:
1. If you watched the videos, please summarize what you have learned and share it with other table members. What are the six tips to persuade people? Which one impresses you the most?
2. Have you used any of the tips or seen anyone using any of them to persuade you before? Were the tips useful?
3. Have you bought anything on TV shopping channels before? Do you think that the TV hosts are good at persuading the audience to buy their products? Which kind of tips do they usually use?
4. If you want to persuade your friends to attend Royal English Club, which tips can be used? Why??
