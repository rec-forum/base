Session 1, Chinese New Year
https://www.cnet.com/how-to/lunar-new-y ... explained/

1, This is the Year of Tiger. What do you expect of this New Year?
2, How did you spend your Chinese New Year holiday last week?
3, What’s your favorite animal?
4, Do you believe the year of the animal will affect a person’s life or personality? Give an example? How about western zodiac signs?
(The Chinese zodiac: Rat, Ox, Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon, Snake, Horse, Sheep, Monkey, Rooster, Dog and Pig. 鼠、牛、虎、兔、龍、蛇、馬、羊、猴、雞、狗、豬)
(The western zodiac: Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, Sagittarius, Capricorn, Aquarius, and Pisces. 白羊座、金牛座、雙子座、巨蟹座、獅子座、處女座、天秤座、天蠍座、射手座、摩羯座、水瓶座和雙魚座。)

Session 2 ‘impromptu discussion + impromptu speech’
For impromptu discussion time (40 minutes), each person prepares one or two questions for the small group discussion.
For impromptu speech time (20 minutes), the host will pick some people to answer his questions. The purpose of impromptu speech (Table topic) is to train the speaker to give a speech without any preparation.
Speaking time for each person is about 2 minutes.
