7/12 (Thur.) Meditation & Yoga/ 10 Ways to have a better conversation (Host: Way)

Posted: Mon Jul 09, 2018 9:57 pm
by Jason Tang
It’s my honor to be host this Thursday 7/12. Many topics discussed in our club before are more focused on News/knowledge/Emotion/Philosophy/habit etc which really enrich our life and broaden our view and lead us toward healthier direction. Today, I choose softer topic “Meditation & Yoga” which make us healthy mentally and physically according to my experience. Let’s discuss this soft topic in session one. Session 2, let’s discuss 10 ways to have a better conversation. Listening to Ted speech is my recommended way to improve your English listening skill. Keep going!

Session I: Meditation & Yoga

Meditation can change Brain?
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W6WvLU5-Cu0&app=desktop

What are the Benefits of Yoga?
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CU3HuPNsyG4

Questions:
1. How much do you know about “Meditation”? Does Meditation really change our brain as Ted mentioned?
2. Can Meditation make our mind calm? Any experience do you have that you can share?
3. How much do you know about Yoga?
4. Have you played yoga before? What’s benefits yoga bring to you?
5. What kind of Yoga have you played? What kind of posture do you think is great?

Session II: 10 Ways to have a better conversation
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R1vskiVDwl4

Questions:
1. What do you think “effective communication” means to you? Is that important in our daily life?
2. Do you face any conversation problems in your communication with others? How do you overcome these problems?
3. Do you have other recommended ways to improve communication quality besides these tips mentioned in “TED”? Or which point do you agree most in the speech?
4. Do you think current communication between people become better or worse after new technology is invented?
5. Do you have any story about misunderstanding in your past by poor conversation? No matter what language spoken is, share with us.
