Session I

What women like in men? 7 Qualities in men that women find attractive
https://bornrealist.com/7-qualities-men-women-find-attractive/

Luckily for you, many scientists have wondered what women want. And they’ve done studies to figure it out. Here are seven traits that researchers have concluded that women find irresistible.

1. Good Looks
Scientists have found that good looks can give you a good head start, but they’re not as important as you think. Studies have shown that women choose better looking guys for short-term flings but not long-term relationships.

2. Selflessness
For Serious Relationships, Studies found that women value selflessness over good looks. Sometimes selflessness is seen as a sign on emotional intelligence. For many of us this one is probably surprising.

3. George Clooney Effect
Women may like older men because they’ve had time to accumulate more resources. Researchers call it the “George Clooney Effect“. So according to this, your chances are high if you’re older or generally more “mature looking”. Seems pretty logical.

4. Sense of Humor
A sense of humor is important to women, it’s believed to be seen as a sign of intelligence. Humor is also a sign of confidence. Nothing new right?

5. Dogs
Small studies have found that the men with dogs do better with the ladies. Researchers believe it makes them seem nurturing. As weird as it sounds this is just how it is.

6. Speed Dating Experiment
In one speed dating experiment, Women said: they were more attracted to men who were mindful, present, and non-judgmental.”
Now who likes judgmental people? I’m pretty sure many people wouldn’t disagree on this point. As for being mindful and present, well those are qualities we all can keep in mind. So next time, be sure to be focused okay?

7. Hero in Action
Women rate higher who take heroic and sometimes primal risks like saving lives, hunting for food, and playing with fire. Even though this idea is dissed by many people (ehm feminists), it has some strong evidence to support it and from time to time we have seen this to be true.

Discussion questions:
1. According to the article above, do you agree with it at all? Which one do you think is the most important quality? ( good looking, a sense of humor, kind, wealthy, animal lover, and heroic) Why?
2. Do you think “playing hard to get could be an effective tactic”?
3. Do you think it’s important to dress well and look good no matter where you are?
4. What are some ways that men can actually impress women? Share your own experience.

Session II

My most embarrassing moment
From Chicken Soup for the Soul
http://www.chickensoup.com/book-story/51715/my-most-embarrassing-moment

1. Once when I was with my first boyfriend, we decided to go to the movies. I didn’t bring any money with me because I thought that he was paying for me. When he didn’t buy my ticket, I casually asked him to lend me five dollars. He told me that he didn’t bring any more money and he sounded annoyed. I ended up calling my mom and asking her to com and pick me up! We broke up the next week.

2. At school in science class, I was the only girl in a group of all boys working on a project. They started bugging me, trying to find out who I liked. I was really annoying and I finally screamed out, “I love Weston!!!!” Everyone in the whole class heard me. Luckily, my crush was sick at the time.

3. I walked right into a flagpole while walking to the bus at school. I was saying good-bye to my friend and when I turned around, I hit it. Everyone was laughing at me so I started laughing, too. I guess now I should listen when someone says to watch where you’re walking.

Discussion questions:
1. We’ve all had embarrassing moments in our life. Please take turns to give a 3-minute presentation about what’s your most embarrassing thing that happened to you.
2. Or you can tell a funny joke.
