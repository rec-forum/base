Session I. What's embarrassed you the most?

https://youtu.be/K7C77zkORUM

What's embarrassed you the most?

I think everybody did have some awkward moment that you felt so embarrassed, even after a long time we still can remember how hard that we felt at the moment. Can we talk this matter just like a funny thing in your life, absolutely no offense intended to anyone. So, please share something you felt that always unforgettable to you.

For instance: my experience
Once, I wore a pair of sandals that I hadn't worn in two or three years. When I walked about 2 minutes away, the soles began to fall off. At first, I thought I could use them reluctantly, but then it became more and more serious. They were all taken off, so I had no choice but to take off my shoes and walk on barefoot. Fortunately, there was a supermarket nearby. I went in and bought a quick glue, which stuck to the soles of my shoes. Fortunately, I could barely wear them back. Change my another shoes at home. However, it was very hot that day, and my bare feet were burnt painfully on the asphalt. I can only walk by jumping, which is really embarrassing. I can still remember it vividly until now.

Questions:
1.Do you still remember the embarrassed event, what caused it happened? What’s the end?
2.Have you ever seen someone had happened an embarrassed thing or accident even? Would you give a hand helping them out?
3.Do you have any interesting story about embarrassed things that you know of?
4.How about what is most proud thing you have done? please share with us.

Section II: Why Are Dogs “Man’s Best Friend”?

https://youtube.com/watch?v=4BvmQmTyCvk&feature=share

There is a legend that after God created human beings, because human beings reproduced too many, he had no time to take care of all people, so he created a dog and let him be a friend of human beings and accompany them by their side.

if you don’t believe, read God in the opposite direction and it becomes dog.
Of course, it's just a legend.


Questions:
1. Do you like dogs, or you don't? please tell us your reason?
2. Scientists find that dogs can be used to comfort traumatized people, or Dogs can help detect drugs or landmines, have you ever views on this kind of report?
3. Have you ever keep dogs as your pet? can you tell us about it?
4. There are many stray dogs, do you have any opinion to help them?
5. Did you ever hear about faithful dogs and their masters touching stories?
6. What benefits can domesticate dogs provide to our families?
7.what kinds of animals is your favorite? tell us why? or travel abroad at post-pandemic era?
