Session I : Are you burning out?

https://au.reachout.com/articles/quiz-a ... urning-out

1.Do you feel drained, run down and generally uninterested in life? What is it like and how do you solve the problem?

2.Normally, what will make you feel burning out? (work, study, hobbies, relationships, mortgage, finance…), Do you have any experience with that? Can you share your feeling with your group members?

3.There is a quiz (test) in the article, what is the result of your quiz? (burn out, going to burn out, ….)? Will you change your life style or adjust something in our life for avoiding burn out? What will you do?

4.Any tip to prevent burning out or depressed? Please share with us.

Session 2: Brand name and Generic

https://tw.voicetube.com/videos/38411

1.People often stereotype generic brands as less tasty, of poorer quality, or less effective than their brand name counterparts. How do you think that people have this stereotype? Do you also have this stereotype? Why or why not?

2.When you want to purchase groceries (coffee, tissue paper, can food….), you are according to the brand name, people’s recommendation, price, discount …? Please share your opinions with your group members.

3.Have you ever purchased something with good brand name but the quality or price doesn’t worth it? What will you do? (don’t buy it next time, tell the store clerk, write the letter to the company….)

4.Where do you usually purchase things/groceries? (from convenient store, supermarket, website…), How do you think of the quality?

5.For the medicine, generic drugs are the same as the brand name. Do you think both of their effects are the same? Have you ever tried generic drugs that you think the effects are totally different from the brand name? Can you share your opinions?
