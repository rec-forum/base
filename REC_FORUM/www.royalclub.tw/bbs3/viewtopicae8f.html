Session I
Dozens children could die from COVID in Taiwan
https://www.taiwannews.com.tw/en/news/4513313

Questions
1. Our government is proud of its advanced deployment and sufficient medical resources. Do you know why the pandemic is still out of control in Taiwan and the death rate quite high?
2. Have you or your relatives and friends ever been infected with COVID, please share the experience from raid antigen test to recovery, and are you satisfied with all the measures by government provided?
3. At this battle fighting against the pandemic in Taiwan, do you think who is worthy to praise or review, why?
4. As the pandemic begins to subside around the world and Taiwan will also slow down in a few months. To sum up Taiwan’s anti- pandemic, do you think is a success or failure, why?



Session II
The Danger of Comparing Yourself to Others
https://fs.blog/comparing-yourself-others/

Questions
1. Have you ever compared yourself to others before? Does it have positive or negative effect on you?
2. If comparing yourself to others is not good, do you know how to stop it?
3. Do you agree with “The most important things in life come from the inside, not the outside” this statement, and why?
4. Why you should be yourself and how to be yourself?
