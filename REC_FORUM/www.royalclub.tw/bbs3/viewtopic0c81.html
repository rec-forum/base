Session One: Self-hypnosis

Please click the following link to read the article.
https://www.healthline.com/health/is-hypnosis-real

Q1. Do you think hypnosis is real or fake? Why? How about meditation and mindfulness? Have you tried any of them?
Q2.Many athletes use hypnosis to improve performance, and release anxiety. Do you think it’s more effective than the placebo?
Q3. “Beauty is in the eye of the beholder.” People are easily hypnotized by their lovers and become better.
Do you think self-hypnosis is a good tool to improve yourself?
Q4. Hypnosis is not “mind-controlled”, no one can be hypnotized against their will. If it’s safe and effective, are you willing to try it?


Session Two: Your body language may shape who you are

Please click the following link to watch the video.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ks-_Mh1QhMc&t=686s

Q5. Do you think your body language may change your mind, and feelings? Can you share your experiences?
Q6. According to the video, we smile when we are happy, and we are happy when we smile. Do you think
“笑笑功” could be an effective way to help us feel happy?
Q7. Can power posing for a few minutes really change your life in meaningful ways? If so, are you willing to
try high power poses to build your confidence when having job interview or standing on stage?
