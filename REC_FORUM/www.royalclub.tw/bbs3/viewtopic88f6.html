Session 1: Summary on Robert Kiyosaki’s bestselling book Rich Dad, Poor Dad
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TcNpoc-lF0M

Discussion
1.According to the video, why many people work very hard, but they never seem to earn enough?
2.Robert Kiyosaki draws a conclusion in his book Rich Dad, Poor Dad that it’s wrong everybody should go to school, get high grades and then get a good job. Do you agree or disagree with his statement? Why or why not?
3.What’s the best piece of advice you’ve received on finance and investing?
4.Where do you struggle when it comes to finance, business, or investing? If you’re not currently struggling, what was your past struggle?
5.The video shows the rich buy assets (things that put $ in your pocket and the poor acquire liabilities (things that take $ out of your pocket). What assets do you plan to buy? What liabilities you tend to reduce?
6.As per the video, it’s not a good idea to buy a house. Do you agree? Why or why not?
7.What’s the best part of what you do?
8.If a young person you really cared about asked you for a piece of financial advice, what advice would you give them?
9.Why do the rich keep getting richer?
10. What’s your best takeaway from this video?


Session 2: High heels for working women -- good or bad?
Women in Japan are fed up with high heels. Actress Yumi Ishikawa started a movement to end this. Her movement means "shoe pain". She tweeted that companies in Japan should not tell women to wear high heels. She also started an online petition. It asks Japan's government to stop companies asking women to wear high heels.
Ishikawa's tweet spread across Asia. She said many women feel like she does, so she started her movement. Many women complain about sore feet, back pain and other issues. A visually impaired woman said it is difficult for her to keep her balance in high heels. Many women who work in Tokyo wear high heels once a week.

Discussion
1.Why do you think companies require women to wear high heels?
2.Have you ever been fed up with your shoes?
3.Should high heels have a health warning?
4.Are employers who require women to wear high heels bad?
5.Have you ever signed an online petition?
6.What advice do you have for women who have to wear heels?
7.Why don't men wear high heels?
8.What are the dangers of high heels?
9.Are high heels worth the pain?
10.Should all workers wear the most comfortable footwear?
