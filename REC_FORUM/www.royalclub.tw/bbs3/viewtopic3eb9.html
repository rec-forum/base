Session 1: Religious Cult: The Story of FLDS and its Leader: Warren Jeffs
https://www.esquire.com/uk/culture/a401 ... weet-pray/

Questions:

1. Have you ever heard about any mysterious religious cult (local or abroad), what do you think about them?
2. How come are these believers so gullible? In your point of view.
3. Fragile minds are vulnerable to injuries and that’s when the evil takes advantage on. What should we do to prevent people from falling into pitfalls of devils?
4. What is the similarity between a religious cult and autocracy in ruling the public?


Session 2: Are you lonely in a crowd?
https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/e ... /ep-230323

Questions:

1. Do you find correlation between this article and the one in session 1?
2. When people get together in a crowd, they become irrational, why?
3. You must have participated in any kinds of group activities before. What kind of activity gives you the feeling that you are part of them and share the same value? What made you feel lonely?
4. Do you feel alone, awkward and alienated in a big crowd? What would you do to improve it (if you need to stay for some reason)?
