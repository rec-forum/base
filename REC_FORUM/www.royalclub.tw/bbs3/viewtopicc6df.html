Session 1 - The Science of Procrastination - And How To Manage It
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1nBwfZZvjKo

Discussion
1. Are you a person who like to complete your mission by procrastination? What reasons cause you not to be on track all the time?
2. Do you think removing temptations around you can prevent procrastination? What do you think procrastination can be avoided?
3. What do you think “No pressure, no progress”? Decent pressure can make people move forward than no pressure at all?
4. Please share your tips with your members- how to manage procrastination?

Session 2 - Why do you always feel tired?
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nn42RC1zT_A

Discussion
1. What’s your sleep quality? Do you sleep well every night?
2. If not, what factors make your sleep be in bad shape?
3. Dose caffeine help you to be energetic? Any other stuff makes you wake up?
4. Any suggestion do you have to share with your members to improve sleep quality?
5. Are you a night owl? Do you like to be a night owl or forced to be?
6. How do you overcome physical or emotional fatigue and keep energetic in most time?
