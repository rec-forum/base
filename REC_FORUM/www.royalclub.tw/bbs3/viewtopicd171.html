Dear Members,
Please see below Youtube website and share you’re your opinion

Session I

How to speak so that people want to listen

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eIho2S0ZahI

Questions:
1. How to make a powerful speech? Any points need to be addressed?
2. What kind of symptom can deter good listening? Do you agree with youtube content? Gossip/judging/negativity/complaining/excusing/lying/dogmatism
3. How to improve your audience listening? Do you agree with youtube content? HAIL (honesty, authenticity, Integrity, Love)
4. How to make your speaking voice more attractive? Do you agree with youtube content? (use skill like Register/timbre/prosody/pace/pitch/volume)
5. Powerful speaking becomes conscious listening as content addressed, mutual understanding is the key to improve communication, do you agree?

Session II

Please take turns to make a practice speech based on the tips offered in Youtube
1. Topic: what benefits you get from attending Royal club? Or any topic you like
2. 3-5 mins for each speaker
