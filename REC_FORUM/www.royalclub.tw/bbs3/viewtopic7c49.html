At the presentation, the time limit is 1-2 minutes for each speaker.
Session 1: What do you think of the Olympic game in Tokyo?
Please keep following the news about the Olympic game and share your comment.
https://focustaiwan.tw/sports/202108015001
1. What do you think of the Olympic game this time?
2. Which competition is the most exciting to you?
3. Did you learn any inspirational story about our Olympic athlete? Please share.
4. What do you think we can improve our Olympic athlete training?

Second session is ‘Table topic’.
First, the host will start asking one question and point at someone to answer it. After answering it, the person can ask another question, and he has the right to assign someone else to answer his question. It’s like a relay race. So please, each participant prepares some questions in his mind. The questions can be about Olympic game, any sport, daily life, current news, education, hobby, science, family, career, love relationship, -------. Speaking time will be strictly controlled. Each answer is for 2 minutes
