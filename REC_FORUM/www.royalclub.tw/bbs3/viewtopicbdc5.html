Session 1 : Everything to know about ‘Triple Stimulus Vouchers’ plan
https://chinapost.nownews.com/20200630-1460030

「振興三倍券」相關單字解析：
stimulus voucher 振興券
Triple Stimulus Vouchers 振興三倍券

triple (adj.) 三倍的
stimulus (n.) 刺激品，激勵物
voucher (n.) 現金卷，代金券，票券

▸People can go to the post offices to buy the stimulus vouchers.
▸The government hopes that the stimulus vouchers will boost domestic consumptions.

stimulate (v.) 激發，激勵；促進
▸The tax cuts will stimulate consumption.
boost (v.) 改善；提高；增強；推動
▸The government managed to boost consumptions by the tax cuts.

其他倍數怎麼說：
兩倍 double
三倍 triple
四倍 quadruple
五倍 quintuple

( https://www.takospace.com/main/ChannelA ... #gsc.tab=0)

另外，也可以使用times去表示：
5倍 five times/5 times
10倍 ten times/10 times
100倍 one hundred times/100 times
