Session 1, mental health
Emotion plays an important part in our health.
https://www.betterhealth.vic.gov.au/hea ... e-emotions

1, Do you have any negative emotion? What? Why? Share one that happened recently.
2, How do you think negative emotion affect your body, mind and spirit? Share your experience.
3, What do you usually do to keep a healthy mind and mood?
4, If you have any friend, family member, colleague who often give you negative energy, what would you do?

Session 2 ‘impromptu discussion + impromptu speech’
For impromptu discussion time (40 minutes), each person prepares one or two questions for the small group discussion.
For impromptu speech time (20 minutes), the host will pick some people to answer his questions. The purpose of impromptu speech (Table topic) is to train the speaker to give a speech without any preparation.
Speaking time will be strictly controlled. Each answer is for 2 minutes.
