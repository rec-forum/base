Session 1. Peyo the horse visits sick people
https://youtu.be/XypgQoOBuBk
https://youtu.be/HrDsVAlGI_s

Questions:
1. Do you think some animals have a unique sense of spirituality with human beings like Peyo. Please share your experiences or the story heard.

2. What do you think about pet therapy? do you think it really gives benefits to patients?

3. If you are a director of a hospital in Taiwan, would you allow a trained horse to join the therapy team as a company for patients interested? And why?

4. Have you or your relative/ friend had the experience of staying in a hospital for treatment? How did they overcome their fear or uncertainty?

Session 2. Happiest & healthiest people on this planet, curious case of Hunza tribe!
https://zeenews.india.com/photos/world/ ... be-2357101
Hunza Valley View~ https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F8cYhngAZfI&t=665s

Questions:
1. Please use your own words to describe why most Hunza tribe people can live up to 100 years old?

2. What do you think that except for Hunza tribe people why in other tribes or developed countries people can't live as long as the Hunza tribe people?

3. If you have to choose between 1. being a rich person, 2. living a long and happy life in Hunza village, and 3. being an ordinary free person, which one would you choose and why?

4. Conversation practice:
In your subgroup, each person please think of a question related to this article then ask your fellow group members.
