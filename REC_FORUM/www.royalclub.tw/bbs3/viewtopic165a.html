Session I Declutter your life

DECLUTTER YOUR LIFE » 10 questions to ask yourself
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8poA6VWb140

Can decluttering your house really make you happier?
https://www.bbc.com/future/article/2019 ... -spark-joy

Q1: Do you personally know someone who has difficulty throwing away their stuff and always keeps piles of clutter in their home? What are the possible reasons for it?

Q2: Do you always “let go” of your surplus belongings you won’t use anymore (from commodities to mementos like photos, books, handwritten cards)? If not, why do you keep them? Have you ever felt regret for having thrown off some of your belongings? What are they and why?

Q3: Following the previous question, can collecting these belongings makes our lives better? Can we be better off with less stuff? What are the advantages/disadvantages to a simpler and tidier lifestyle?

Q4: Are you good at decluttering your home? Please share your ways and tips with us.


Session II Declutter your mind

How To Declutter Your Mind: 10 Practical Tips You'll Actually Want To Try
https://www.forbes.com/sites/nomanazish ... e7e8424f15


Q5. Have you ever experienced negative thinking in your mind, such as self-pity, self-doubt, worry and rumination that are not helpful to problem solving. What are they and why did they happen?

Q6: Following the previous question, how do you push those disturbing thoughts out of your head?

Q7: Do you ever feel like your mind is full of messy work and life tasks, which hinder you from stay focus? What are they?

Q8: The article provides 10 practical tips to help us declutter our mind. Which do you think is most helpful? Do you have any other suggestions?
