7. platitude: n. 陳詞濫調

14. spinster: n. 未婚女子
15. buck up: 振作
16. grump: n. 脾氣壞的人

18. devastating: a. 辛辣的，挖苦的

Discussion questions
1. Why are some compliments considered actually insulting? Do they have common factors?
2. What are some other examples of “insulting” compliments?
3. Has anybody you know been given “insulting” complements? Or have you given one to your friend? How does the person deal with this embarrassing moment?
4. Please think up some ways or smart lines to answer to the above mentioned “insulting” compliments or give some suggestions to avoid embarrassing moments.
