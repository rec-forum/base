Session I : Why You Should Be Wary About Incredible Online Shopping Deals

https://tw.voicetube.com/videos/167860

2.When you purchase things from websites, do you use the search engine (suck like feebee, findprice, 比價網) to find the cheapest one first? Or how do you usual search the thing which you want to buy on the website? Please share your experience.
3.The article mentioned “When we find something we really want, our judgment is impaired. That's no accident”. Do you agree with it? Why or why not? Any experience to share with?
4.Have you ever been scammed from the website purchasing? What did you react or deal with the scam?
