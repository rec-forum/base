Topic I: Life Contract

While alive, preparing for future events in the future, looking for the ideal life etiquette business, the contract between the two parties on related matters. The meaning of the “life contract” is that the parties can plan their subsequent events in advance, and allow family members and relatives to return to normal life as soon as possible after the party’s death. The purpose of the “life contract” is to distribute the current economic pressure of life and reduce the emotional impact of relatives and to fulfill family responsibilities and personal dignity by means of appointment specifications and prepayments and installments.

The characteristics of the “life contract” are: cost transparency, oversight agency, performance guarantee, 75% of the contract money is delivered to the trust, can be terminated halfway, information is transparent, and customer subjectivity.

And the functions of the “life contract” are:
1.From the perspective of risk avoidance, it can be said that it is a cost to plan for a wind that will inevitably occur.
2.From the perspective of life management, it provides users with the right to arrange their own affairs and maintains the user’s dignity before and after the end of life.
3.From the perspective of financial planning, the concept of appointments can avoid the cost increase caused by the inflation.
4.From the perspective of the care of surviving family, by properly arranging their own affairs, relatives can not cause emotional shock and economic crisis due to their past lives, which is also a manifestation of love and responsibility.

Questions:
1.Do you agree with live contract? Why?
2.Explain the functions of live contract.
3.What can a life contract do for life?
