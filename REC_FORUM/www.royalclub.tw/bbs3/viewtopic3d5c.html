Articlle 1 ~~
Fukushima child statue: Residents complain about radiation suit

https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-45179095

Discussions~

1) Why Fukushima residents reacted angrily to the statue in their city and demanded to remove it?

2) Can you describe the Fukushima’s disaster?

3) Fukushima disaster radiation still continues to contaminate underground water? Do you think nuclear disaster is still having an impact today? Why?

4) From the Chernobyl & Fukushima’s disaster, do you trust the nuclear energy supply?

5) Nuclear is potentially threatened by tsunamis, why do many countries keep pushing nuclear energy?

6) Only days after the nuclear meltdown in Fukushima, German government previously defended nuclear energy and gradually turn off all nuclear power plants forever as well as coal in the future. Do peoples need to fear that abandoning nuclear energy could occur another disaster?

Article 2
'My robot makes me feel like I haven't been forgotten'
https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/business-45332710

Discussions~

1) What’s the Telepresence robot?

2) How AV1 robot can help sick kids take part in lessons & helps them see their friends?

3) Do you think robot can be a pet? Will robot helps us happier instead of loneliness?

4) Robot can do everything in the near future; does it mean we will have more time to do what we want it?

5) Robots may soon be looking after grandma – or sharing the bed. Please share some of the other unique impacts in the future.

6) Robots learn to live in human society, or human learn to live as robots?
