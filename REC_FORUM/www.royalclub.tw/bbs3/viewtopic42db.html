Article1: Christmas Eve Traditions and Customs
https://www.whychristmas.com/customs/christmaseve.shtml

1. Do you celebrate Christmas Day or Christmas eve? Please share your special experiences.
2. Have you ever been to any other countries to see how they celebrate Christmas?
3. What do you think about the foreign festival in Taiwan?
4. Don’t forget the traditional holiday in December is called Winter Solstice. Did you eat the glutinous dumplings this year?*Happy Winter Solstice!*


Article2: Learn to talk about retail therapy in 6 minutes
https://tw.voicetube.com/videos/57928

1. Do you agree that shopping is therapeutic?
2. Did you ever try the retail therapy to cheer yourself up?
3. If yes, what kind of things you usually buy?
4. Did you feel better or feel regret?
