1/25--11 Crazy Ways The Weather Influences Your Health and Behavior/ Understanding Foreign Cultures By Eating Their Food

Posted: Sun Jan 21, 2018 7:50 am
by Jason Tang
Host: Johnny

Session I
11 Crazy Ways The Weather Influences Your Health and Behavior
https://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/weather-and-health_us_570facd9e4b08a2d32b92ae9

Discussion:
1. Please name one or two points of view in this article that you could not agree with more and explain why.
2. Do you or your family members have any health problems when weather changes? (Such us sneezing, stuffy nose, chapped lips, achy joints, headache, high/low blood pressure and so on.)
3. Are you a hot weather person or a cold weather lover? Why? (What are your favorite and least favorite seasons of the year? What makes it unique or different from other seasons to you?)

Session II
Understanding Foreign Cultures By Eating Their Food
http://www.newsweek.com/understanding-foreign-cultures-eating-their-food-92593

Discussion:
1. What is the food style that you most prefer, Chinese, Japanese, Thai, or western style?
2. Do you trust in Michelin Stars? Have you ever try the restaurant that Michelin guide to recommend to you? Please share your opinion or experience.
3. Food culture depends on what is the crops we plant, so do you believe in it shape us to be different with western people even the Africa people?
4. In many cases, foreign cuisine would change the flavor to makes acceptable to local. How about you? Do you like to taste the original or the changed flavor?
5. Do you think the night market foods can represent Taiwan's food feature? Please share your thought with everyone.
