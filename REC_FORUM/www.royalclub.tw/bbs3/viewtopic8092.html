by Jason Tang
Session I: What's Your Biggest Regret? (Nobody Wants to Admit the Worst One of All)
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R45HcYA8uRA

Discussion questions:
1. Do you regret something you have ever done or never done? What are they?

2. Why do you think people have regrets in the first place? Explain.

3. Should we live with no regrets completely or learn how to somehow live with our regrets?

4. What’s the best way to make regrets hurt less? Or how do you make regrets go away?

5. What’s the one thing you would change in your life if you could travel back in time?


Session II: Tidying Up with Marie Kondo & Hoarders

Tidying Up with Marie Kondo Official trailer (HD)/Netflix
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WvyeapVBLWY
What Turns People into Hoarders?
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yaxHU7LHJt0

Discussion questions:
1. How tidy are you? Do you agree with Marie Kondo’s method of “tidying up”?

2. Do you have a hard time getting rid of stuff? Please share your experience.

3. What causes someone to be a hoarder?

4. How do you deal with a hoarder?

5. What is hoarding disorder? Is hoarding a mental health problem?
