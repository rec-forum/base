Session 1 doing detox
https://www.verywellmind.com/why-and-ho ... ox-4771321
Questions for discussion
1. Do you think digital detoxing is necessary? Are you suffering from digital toxin?
2. What kind of digital toxin do you want to avoid?
3. What do you think of technology and cell phone?
4. What do you think is the most benefit you get from Internet and the harm hard to shake off?
5. Do you spend too much time on cell phone or Internet?


Session 2 ‘impromptu discussion + impromptu speech’
For impromptu discussion time (40 minutes), each person prepares one or two questions for his group discussion.
For impromptu speech time ( 20 minutes), the host will prepare some questions to ask. (assign someone to answer or may ask a volunteer’. The purpose of impromptu speech (Table topic) is to train the speaker to give a speech without any preparation.
Speaking time will be strictly controlled. Each answer is for 2 minutes.
