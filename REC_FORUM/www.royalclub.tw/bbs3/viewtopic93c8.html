How to stop bickering and nagging ?
https://youtu.be/FHxFIRaAV54

1. It's so annoying when you have to say the same thing over and over again. Why people like to nag ?
2. Will the nagging stop if you just do what you're asked of ?
3. Nagging is the desire to change other people. Can it be effective ?
4. No one like to be a nagger, but they often feel like they have no choice. Do you agree with it.
5. Do you have any tips to stop nagging ?
6. Nagging someone is one of the most toxic things you can do for a person self isteems, along with bullying. Do you agree with it ?

----------------------------------------------
How worried are you ?
https://youtu.be/q1FQzNiA8vI

1. Do you consider yourself to be a worrier ?
2. Do yo have a hard time controlling your worry ?
3. Do you worry to avoid bad things from happening ?
4. When you are stressed and worried, what do you tend to dream about at night ?
5. Are you worried about your life, what you will eat or about your body, what you will put on .
6. How can you change your thinking --worry is tutile, worry is fruitless and worry is inappropriate ?
