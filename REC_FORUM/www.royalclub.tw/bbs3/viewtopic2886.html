Session 1
Know your enemy: Rise of the Roaches
https://blog.rentokil.co.za/know-your-enemy-cockroaches

1. Do you have any problems with cockroaches or ants or any other insects in your house? What do you usually do with them and how effective is it? Can you completely eradicate them?
2. Why are so many people afraid of cockroaches? Are you scared of it? How scared are you? Can you share your experience?
3. We know cockroaches are one of the most difficult pests to eradicate, so if the house is kept clean all the time, will there be no cockroaches? Is this true? Why?
4. Do you think it’s wrong to be afraid of cockroaches if one is a man? We know some phobias are genetic, can we overcome them and why?

Session 2
7 Ways: Home Cooking Beats Ordering and
Takeout
https://www.healthline.com/health/food- ... us-takeout

1. Cooking at home generally can save a lot of time and money, and is more hygienic and healthy, is this true and why? Please share your experience.
2. If you have time, will you cook at home or eat out? Why?
3. Can you cook and enjoy it? Do you think preparing meals for your family is an easy job for you or not? Why?
4. Do you bring your lunch on the day at work or just eat out? What are pros and cons?
5. Do you think your husband should learn how to cook and why?
