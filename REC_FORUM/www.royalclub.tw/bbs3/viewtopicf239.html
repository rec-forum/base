Session 1: 2023 will be the year of aftershocks and unpredictability, according to The Economist's The World Ahead 2023
https://finance.yahoo.com/news/2023-aft ... 00051.html

Questions:
1. As the author said in this article, there are ten themes for 2023. Which one do you concern the most and why? Which one will impact Taiwan the most?
2. Other than these ten themes, can you think of another unpredictable issue for 2023?
3. In your opinion, which one of these themes could be avoided from happening? Why?
4. Are you more optimistic or pessimistic for next year?

Session II: Retrospect and Prospect
In this session, members in the small group will take turns asking question to the group, all the members in that group need to answer that question in maximum 3 mins, including the person who asks the question. This is to make sure each person has the opportunity to ask and answer the question and have fair amount of time to speak.
When it is your turn to ask question, you can pick a question from the provided questions as below or just create any question you want to ask.

Questions:
1. Which things made you a great sense of accomplishment in 2022?
2. Have you regretted not fully doing, being or having something in 2022?
3. What have you tried something new in 2022?
4. What do you wish you spend more time doing for next year?
5. What is the one thing you would most like to change yourself for next year?
