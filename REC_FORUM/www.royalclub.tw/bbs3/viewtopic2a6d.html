First session: One Thing You Can Do: Make Your New Year’s Resolutions count
(From The New York Times)

https://www.nytimes.com/2020/01/01/clim ... works.html

Questions:

1. Why should we care about what happens to the polar ice caps?
2. What climate change impacts have you noticed?
3. How does climate change affect local plants and trees?
4. Will the remedies to climate change be worse than the disease? Will it drive more people into poverty with higher costs?
5. What can you do as individual to slow down climate change?
