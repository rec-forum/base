[Session I: How AI can save our humanity (Kai-Fu Lee | TED2018)
https://www.ted.com/talks/kai_fu_lee_how_ai_can_save_our_humanity?utm_source=tedcomshare&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=tedspread

Discussion Questions:
1. What kind of jobs will be replaced by AI in near future? What jobs will still be around in 20 years? Please share your thoughts.
2. From this speech, we learned “How AI and mankind can coexist”. Please share 3 points of your learning or thoughts with your team members.
3. What do you think that people will regret most about your life when your time is up? Failing to fulfill your duty and obligations? Or failing to follow your dreams? Please share some stories or your comments at your table.
4. How do you arrange your work-life balance? Please share your experiences or suggestions with your team members.

Session II: How to build (and rebuild) trust (TED2018 Frances Frei)
https://www.ted.com/talks/frances_frei_how_to_build_and_rebuild_trust/transcript

Discussion Questions:
1. Had you ever experienced “trust is broken” in your past life? Please share your stories or the stories you learned at your table.
2. In this video, it mentioned “And if you do nothing else, please put away your cell phone.” It is the largest distraction magnet yet to be made, and it is super difficult to create empathy and trust in its presence.” Do you agree it or not? Why?
3. Please share” 4 Ways to Communicate Effectively “with your table members.
4. If trust is broken in a relationship, can it be repaired? How to rebuild trust? Please share your comments from this video with your table members.
