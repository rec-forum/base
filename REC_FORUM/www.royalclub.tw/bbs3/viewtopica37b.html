Session 1
Stop to be a people- pleaser. https://www.verywellmind.com/how-to-sto ... er-5184412

1. Do you think a people- pleaser is ok or not, why or why not?
2. Do you think a people pleaser is happy or actually not? Why?
3. How much are you as a people- pleaser ranking from 1 to 10? ( to your spouse, to your mom-in-law, to your boss, to your teachers, to your friends, classmates, to the tradition ----). What’s the result?
4. Did you ever think about changing yourself as a people pleaser?
5. Please share your experience or the stories you heard?

Second session is ‘Table topic’.
The purpose of Table topic is to train a speaker to give an impromptu speech.
The host will give an example by asking someone (anyone) to ask him a question. After the host answers the question, he will ask someone else another question. After the person answers it, he will ask another question to someone else. It’s like a relay race. So please, each participant prepares some questions in his mind. The questions can be about daily life, covid 19, cell phones , sport, daily life, current news, education, hobby, science, family, career, love relationship, -------. Speaking time will be strictly controlled. Each answer is for 2 minutes.
