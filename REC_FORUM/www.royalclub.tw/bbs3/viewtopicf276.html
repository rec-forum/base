Session 1: “Midlife- When We Want Happiness Now”

Source: https://www.psychologytoday.com/blog/th ... ss-now?eml

Questions:
1. What stage of life do you think you are in now? How is it different from the previous stage, both inside and outside?

2. Is your identity changed through different stages of your life? How?

3. How do you confront the aging self?

4. What is the un-lived life that is calling out to you, if there is one?



Session 2: How to be happy every day: it will change the world.

Source: https://youtu.be/78nsxRxbf4w

Extended reading: “5 Ways Giving is Good for you”

https://greatergood.berkeley.edu/articl ... od_for_you

Questions:
1. Are you a happy person？What makes you happy?

2. Do you agree giving makes the giver happy？ Why or why not？

3. What do you feel when giving? Give one or two examples.
