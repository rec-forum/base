Session I:
https://stackla.com/resources/blog/cons ... ior-stats/

Questions:
1. What kind of shopping do you enjoy the most, TV shopping, Catalogue shopping, Window shopping, on-site shopping or online shopping? (Can be multiple choice)
2. There is a huge shopping season in the world during the Q4 of every year, from Taiwan’s Anniversary Sale, China’s Double 11 Shopping Festival, USA’s Black Friday and Cyber Monday holiday shopping, to Christmas shopping season (Super Saturday and the Thursday before Christmas).
Which shopping day would you like to join most? Why? Or you have your own way to go shopping? Please share it with us?
3. For the coming shopping season, have you prepared a shopping list? What must-buy items should you put on your shopping list?
4. Do you plan to spend quintuple stimulus vouchers on the items you will buy during shopping?
5. Have you purchased from new brands during online shopping since the pandemic started?

Session II:
Taiwan International Coffee Show 2021
https://www.chanchao.com.tw/coffee/en/

Questions:
1. Have you attended fairs inside Taiwan or international shows outside Taiwan? If yes, please share your experiences. If not, please go to Q2. Here fairs mean exhibitions or expositions.
2. Have you visited shows inside Taiwan or international shows outside Taiwan? If yes, please share your experiences. If not, please go to Q3.
3. Have you visited online fairs or virtual shows? If yes, please share your experiences. If not, please take time to visit the online coffee exhibition on 19-22 Nov., 2021 and share your experience.
4. What are benefits of attending and visiting fairs?
5. Does the post-pandemic have any impact on the effects of joining fairs? Please share your thinking.
