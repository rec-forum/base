Session 1, Trendy tips to organize your closet
https://docs.google.com/document/d/1Fot ... 2cprc/edit

1. According to the article, a well-organized closet means a lot to the start of whole day. Do you agree with that and why?
2. How to tidy up and store has become an important lesson now. Do you have your own principle to manage house storage and purchasing decision?
3. Besides the closet, do you have any experience to share about the space management in your house?
4. Is it easy to say goodbye to the stuff you don’t need in your opinion?


Session 2, The return of the liberal arts major
https://docs.google.com/document/d/1gCM ... GYqkk/edit
1. Artificial intelligence can’t be emphasized more recently. Do you agree with the view of this article that technology-related majors will continue to lose relevance?
2. Can you observe the different characteristics between people with tech and liberal arts degree in your working place or your personal experience?
3. What kind of jobs could most possibly be replaced by AI in near future?
4. After reading this article, will you encourage students in Taiwan to major in liberal arts?
