Host : Danny

Session 1.
Do you hate speaking English around native speakers
Please find video as below.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lawHYX5eB20

Discussions:
1. Do you hate speaking English around native speakers? Are you sometimes getting stammer or losing words while you’re not when speaking with other non-native English learner? Why or why not?
2. In many occasion, some people look at native speaker as if they are up and better than them in anyway, what’s your opinion?
3. What were the longest conversations you have had with foreigner? How was that?
4. it’s not a test but a scenario imagination If you were asked by a foreigner in the airplane about how to make a tour to Taipei, can you do it in no time? If so, show us members how. If not, anything else we should add?
5. Share your now-the-best English learning methods or any textbooks, materials or resources that you can bring in to better our language skills.


Session 2.
Introverts vs Extroverts
Please find video as below.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sfjN15zsPyQI

Discussions:
1. Are you an introvert , extrovert or just right in the middle, ambivert? How do you measure it?
2. Are there any advantage of being an introvert or extrovert? Which one is better? What are the downsides of them?
3. In many ways, our society has an extroversion bias that extroverts can do things much better then introverts, because we’re in a sociable society? What’s your opining?
4. Is it a stereotype or not that introverts are shy and unsociable? Contrary to the perspectives, introverts may even be better public speaking, according to the video .do you agree?
5. Do you know anything about your personality traits? Have you ever tried looking for jobs or finding a mate by that?
