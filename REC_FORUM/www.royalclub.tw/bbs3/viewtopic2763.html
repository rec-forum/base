Session I
Taiwan's 'living hell' traffic is a tourism problem, say critics
https://edition.cnn.com/travel/article/ ... index.html

Discussion:
1. "In Taiwan, the characteristic friendliness of Taiwanese people vanishes as soon as they get behind the steering wheel." Do you have any response to this sentence?
2. "Taiwan is a living hell for pedestrians." Do you agree at this criticism? Are there any cities in the island that can be excluded from it?
3. Taiwan is the country with the highest locomotive density in the world. Why do Taiwan people like to ride motorcycles?
4. My father used to say that motorcycles are suicide cars. What are the dangers of riding a motorcycle?
