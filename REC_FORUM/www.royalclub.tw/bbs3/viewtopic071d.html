Session I
Major drought drives 20M toward hunger in Africa
https://taipeitimes.com/News/world/arch ... 2003777758

Question:
1. How does the drought affect Africans, especially children under the age of six?
2. What is the number of hungry people due to drought through 2022?
3.“Dire conditions in the Horn of Africa have been amplified by Russia’s war in Ukraine, which has contributed to soaring food and fuel costs……” Do you agree or disagree? What are the reasons?
4. Do you agree that extreme weather events are happening with increased frequency and intensity due to climate change? Why?
5. Taiwan recently got the worst drought in 56 years in June 2021. Do you think that it will happen again this year? Why or why not? Is our government well prepared to face the extreme climate?

Session II
A robot lives in this Antarctic penguin colony. It's trying to save them
https://edition.cnn.com/2022/04/29/worl ... index.html

Questions:
1. What features does ECHO have? What does ECHO look like?
2. How does ECHO help the scientists to monitor Antarctic penguins?
3. How do the scientists tag Antarctic penguins? What are the reasons that scientists need to tag them?
4. Why does the number of Antarctic penguins decline rapidly?
5. What kind of indications do Antarctic penguins indicate in the food chains and the environment?
