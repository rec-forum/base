Session I
https://grow.grin.co/should-i-become-a-youtuber/

Questions:
1. What are the pros and cons of being a YouTuber?
2. Which YouTuber do you like most? Why?
3. Do you think being a YouTuber worth it?
4. Do YouTubers have good or bad influence on our society?
5. How do think some people use YouTube channel to lead viewers' thought?

Session II

https://daringtolivefully.com/new-year-resolution-ideas

Questions:

1. Do you fulfill your 2019 resolutions? If yes, congratulations, if not, what reasons hinder you from accomplishing?
2. What are your 2020 new year's resolutions? Any practical plans to execute them?
3. What are some New Year's resolutions that people will break quickly?
4. Is it useful by using dream board or vision board to fulfill our resolutions?
5. Do you have any tips to help people's new year's resolutions succeed?
