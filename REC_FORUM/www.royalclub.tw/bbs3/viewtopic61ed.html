Session 1: Expert lists reasons behind Taiwan ‘s negative population growth
https://www.taiwannews.com.tw/en/news/4101181

Questions:
1. Taiwan’s total population showed negative growth last (2020) year, and this phenomenon may continue to deteriorate. What impact do you think will have on Taiwan’s future or society?
2. Experts point out that the reason for the decline in the birth rate is the falling marriage rate. Do you agree with this statement? If not, what do you think is the real reasons?
3. If life can be repeated, are you willing to get married and have children? Why? Do you still have the traditional idea of passing on from generation to generation?
4. What kind of policies or incentives do you think will make young people want to marry and have children?
5. If compared with your childhood, do you think the children today are very happy and fortunate? Why?
6. There are more and more people who do not want to get married. Do you know the real reason? Share it with us.


Session 2:
Mountains vs Beach --- What Millennials Think
https://www.shethepeople.tv/home-top-vi ... ls-choice/

Questions:
1. Do you agree with the statement that introverts prefer mountains and extroverts prefer beaches? Please share why you like it?
2. It is very convenient to get close to the mountains or beaches in Taiwan. What activities do you usually engage in there?
3. Although a person ‘s personality is introvert and extrovert, each has its advantages and disadvantages, and there is no good or bad. If you can choose, which personality do you like? Why?
