Article 1: 10 Dangerous Food Safety Mistakes
https://www.cdc.gov/foodsafety/ten-dang ... takes.html
Question:
1.What kind of mistake can lead to serious foodborne illness?
2.What things you should never make in your Instant Pot to avoid serious illness, and how to keep yourself safe when preparing this type of food?
3.How long is it safe to eat leftovers? How to tell whether food has gone bad?
4.Is microwaving food safe? Does using a microwave to cook ruin the nutrients in food?
5.Does food cooked in a microwave taste any different from the same food cooked in an oven?


Article 2: 11 Safety Tips to Memorize Before Your Next Flight
https://www.travelandleisure.com/travel ... gency-tips
Question:
1. Do you dare to drink hot water, coffee or tea on a plane? Or do you only drink liquids that come in a can or bottle?
2. What things should we do to be better prepared which will make every flight more pleasant, while improving our odds in the highly unlikely event of an emergency?
3. What to do if an airline damages or loses your luggage?
4.How true is the "last in first out" concept for checked in luggage?
5.Does the priority tag on checked suitcase make a difference? Is it true that the handlers deliberately ignore priority baggage tags?
