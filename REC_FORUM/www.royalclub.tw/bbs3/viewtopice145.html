Session 1
Gout
Its nickname is " disease of kings". Hence Louis XIV in France, 忽必烈 in Yuan Dynasty and Benjamin Franklin ( a founding father of US) suffered from it.
In 2003 the prevalence rate of gout in Taiwan was 3% .It's about 10 times more than USA and Europe.
In 2010 the prevalence had reached first position in the world!

Gout is partly genetic, contributing to about 60% of variability in uric acid level.The SLC2A9, SLC22A12, and ABCG2 genes have been found to be commonly associated with gout and variations in them can approximately double the risk.And authority concerned in Taiwan confirmed
the ABCG2 is Taiwanese gout patients' pathogenic prime gene.
( source from:https://udn.com/news/story/11322/3207434)
