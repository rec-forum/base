Session I : 12 ways to reduce your cancer risk

https://www.cancer.ie/reduce-your-risk/ ... YDnvr.dpbs

Discussion:
Q1: Do you hear any powerful survivor stories of cancer patients? Please share these stories about how their lives have been changed by cancer with your team members.

Q2: This article provides 12 ways to reduce your cancer risk. Which ways do you want to try? Why?

Q3: In this article mentioned that reduce the risk of cancer by ensuring your children take part in vaccination programs for Human Papillomavirus (HPV vaccine) (targeted for girls in first year of secondary school and upwards). Why do you agree your children to take HPV vaccine in school? Why not?

Q4: How do you change your lifestyle and become a healthy person? Please share your comments at your table.


Session II : 10 Benefits of a Good Night's Sleep

https://www.verywellhealth.com/top-heal ... ep-2223766


Q1: How many hours do you need night’s sleep? Having trouble sleeping? Please share your experience with team members.

Q2: How long can you go without sleep? What are the effect of lack sleep?

Q3: Do you have some tips to help you overcome insomnia? Please share your experiences or your learning at your table.

Q4: This article provides 10 benefits of good night’s sleep. Which points do you agree or disagree? Why?
