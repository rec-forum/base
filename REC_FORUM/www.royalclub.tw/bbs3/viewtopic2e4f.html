Topic 1: Does accent matter?
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dPcGhkRgnmU
Q1.Does accent matter? In your opinion, which English accent sound the best? Are you copying American, British or Austrian accent?
Q2. Do you pursue perfect accent? Do you speak with strong accent?
Q3. Are you proud of or ashamed of your accent? Have you struggled to understand some people's English? For example, Indian, Japanese and Filipino usually have strong accents in English?
Q4. Which is more important, accuracy (use the right grammar) or fluency（speak quickly without many pauses）?
Q5. What are the tips to sound more native?

Topic 2: Popular kids
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rTvZtLAOa8k
Q1. While in school, were you a popular kid? Did your try to act cool?
Q2.Do you agree with the study conclusion that popular kids usually end up to be terrible adults?
Q3. Have you had a crush on hot girls or bad boys while in school? Where are they now? As an adult, if you see them now, will you still find them attractive?
Q4. Have your done anything stupid because of peer pressure?
