Session1: How to improve your willpower

https://reurl.cc/OqalMR

Questions:
1. Do you believe that willpower is the key to success? Why? In addition to willpower, what do you think are also the elements of success? What does success mean to you?
2. Willpower is the ability to control yourself. Do you think everyone has this ability and like to use it? Why? If a person lack of willpower, can actually develop and keep it?
3. The article mentioned that building a positive habit is an essential element for success, can you make an example in your daily life why positive habit can help you reach your goal?
4. Do you know what are the bad habits that hold you back from success? If so, have you ever thought about change it? What is the result?

Session2: Three more students dead from apparent suicide

https://focustaiwan.tw/society/202011170017

Questions:
1. Most people think that students are the simplest and most carefree period, why do they choose to commit suicide? Is there something wrong with our society, school or family? Can we provide any assistance for them? Please share your personal opinion?
2. When you face a certain dilemma or setback that cannot be resolved, how can you handle it?
3. A person want to live, which means that he is still full of infinite vision and expectations in life, and hopes to be realized one day, What is your expectations in life? Can you share?
4. According to statistics,76 college students have committed suicide and over 1000 try to so far this year in Taiwan. This may be just the tip of the iceberg. Can you think about what other issues we deserve to pay attention in Taiwan?
