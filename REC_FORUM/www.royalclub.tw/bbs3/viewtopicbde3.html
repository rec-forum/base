2018 Royal club year-end party & installation

Posted: Mon Dec 10, 2018 9:45 pm
by Shuman
(12/8) 21 people, including senior members and new friends, participated in Royal club year end party & installation.
1.we enjoyed delicious food and sang interesting songs together in 星聚點. We found many talented singers in our club.
2.We had a ceremony to appreciate the devotion of Presidents Jason Tang & Shuman ,P.R. teams to the club in 2018. We also introduced and welcomed the 2019 team led by Morris & Danny Lin. It is important to have volunteers to continue to support and manage this club.
3.Every participant made an impressive English speech. We all agreed English is very important, so we appreciated the funder, William, established this club 19 years ago, making us have a good platform to practice English, enrich our knowledge, and meet friends. We all sincerely hoped this club can last forever.
In conclusion, we had a wonderful and unforgettable party, so most of us will keep attending the regular meetings, and looked forward to next outing.
