Topic 1: Pay cut, the way to survive at work for women over 40? ( https://today.line.me/TW/pc/article/ZWJ ... =lineshare)

After reading discussions:
1. Retell the case in English as a group.
2. Do you agree to the idea that Lee Yun, the lady in the case, or any other worker, should offer pay cut to hold onto her jobs? Why or why not?
3. What should Lee Yun do to survive if her friend didn't have any job offer?
4. Why would women's age be such a big deal in local corporate culture?
5. If you were in Lee Yun's shoes, what would you have done differently?
Topic 2: Do zodiac signs reveal who we really are?
https://www.thoughtco.com/your-zodiac-s ... an-1212277

After reading discussions:
1. Do you think our personality is shaped by zodiac signs, or affected by our experiences? In what ways?
2. How has your personality changed since you were out of school?
3. If you could change one aspect of your personality, what would you change?
4. What personality traits do you think are most important for a friend to have? A spouse? A boss? A teacher?
5. Have you ever heard the expression “You can’t judge the book by its cover”? What does it mean?
