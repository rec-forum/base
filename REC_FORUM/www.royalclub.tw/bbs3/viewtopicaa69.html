Session I:
Running Saved My Life and It Might Save Yours Too

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=x_Uw0NiseBM

Points for Discussion:
1. Did you like PE(Physical Education) when you were in adolescence?
How did you go through rebellious stage or emotional disturbance? Do you have any stories to share about dealing with teenagers?

2. The speaker does not like rules. Her teachers called her a bad kid when she was 5 years old. But she created a program on her own experience and helped many girls after many years. What are your opinions about a bad /good kid? Were you a bad /good kid when you were young ?

3. There is no rule for running. How do you think? How do you like running or jogging?

4. Running can releases anxiety. How do you think of happy pace for mood? What would you do to build positive mental health/confidence when you are in a bad mood?

5. Any action or movement starts with one step. What is your one step for specific action?
