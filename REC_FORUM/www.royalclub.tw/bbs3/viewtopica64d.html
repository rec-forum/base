Session I

Three Common Bedtime Habits Destroying Your Sleep
https://elemental.medium.com/3-common-b ... f5fe5e4c08


1. How is your sleep quality? Please rate your sleep quality on a rating scale of 1-10, where 1 is “very poor” and 10 is “very good”.

2. In your opinion, what is high-quality sleep? Is quantity important? How many hours do you usually sleep per day?

3. How do you get a good night’s sleep? Please share some smart habits that improve sleep quality, and some habits that destroy it.



Session II

Night owls and early birds have different personality traits
https://www.cbsnews.com/news/night-owls ... ty-traits/

1. Are you an early bird (or a lark) or night owl? Or something in-between? What time do you usually go to sleep and wake up? Do you take a nap in the daytime?

2. Do you know any “die-hard” late risers or early risers? Do they have different personality traits? Do you believe sleep habits make a difference on personality, intelligence, and/or success rate?

3. Can our sleep habits be changed? Do you believe our inner body clock is ruled by our genetics? Is it possible to make the shift from evening people to morning people (or the opposite)?
