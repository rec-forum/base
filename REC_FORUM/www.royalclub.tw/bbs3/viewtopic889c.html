5/9(Sat.)Board game"Who Is Spy"/Garnishing cocktails with edible insects. Host:Nora
Session 1.
Hello everyone, we had played the classic board game "Werewolves of Miller's Hollow " and this Saturday, we are going to have another popular board game "Who Is Spy".(game description below)

Getting bored of staying home all the time? Come here to join us with your masks on, let's have something new this Saturday.

Session 2.
Garnishing cocktails with edible insects
https://youtu.be/aR-NubbhYco

This video shows that a younger bartender from Singapore uses unusual ingredients to get interesting flavors.

Questions:
1. Have you eaten something unusual? How do you feel? Share your experience
2. If you have chance to taste these special food will you give yourself a try? And why?
3. Do you hear about any other strange food?

Description of Who Is Spy:
In this game, there are two roles, mortals and spies.
Each of you will have a piece of paper, and you are not allowed to show it to anyone, keep it secret.

There is a picture and vocabulary on the paper, try to describe in one sentence without talking about the name.

You will have no idea if you are the spy or not. So pay attention to others' comment.

After a round, participators should vote who is most likely be the spy.
