Session 1: Is cohabitation wrong?

Here are debates over the issue of cohabitation, please check the link below, and pick some opinions for your references.
https://www.debate.org/opinions/is-cohabitation-wrong

Questions:
1. Is cohabitation wrong? If not, please tell your table members why?
2. What are the pros and cons of cohabitation?
3. Do men or women get the most benefits of cohabitation?
4. Many young people now think that marriage costs a lot of money and energy, while cohabitation costs them nothing. Still, some consider cohabitation as a trial when some think it as cheating? Any thoughts about that?
5. As Socrates Quotes. “By all means, marry. If you get a good wife, you'll become happy; if you get a bad one, you'll become a philosopher”. What’s your interpretation about it?

Session 2: Nomophobia

Please find the video below,
http://www.fox5ny.com/279053126-video

Here are some tips for you to see if you have nomophobia?
• You never turn your phone off
• You obsessively check for texts, missed calls and emails
• You always take your phone to the bathroom with you
• You never let the battery run out

Questions:
1. Do you have nomophobia? Any other symptoms you know expecting these above? Do you think it as nothing or could it be something that’s currently affecting your life?
2. Have you ever left your phone behind when you were going to somewhere? Please share you story of how you felt and how you dealt with it?
3. What causes people so obsessive to their smart phone? Try to think of 3 reasons for it and explain why?
4. Let’s put ourselves in a scenario: If you’re a boss leading a company, but you’ve been wondering that your employees are busy for their work or texting their friends? Be honest and put yourself in the boss’s shoes, would you just directly ban them from using social media at office, or Sneaked behind them and see what’s going on? Or just let them use it freely for the concern of privacy?
