Article 1: Taiwan to set up agency promoting bilingual by 2030 initiative
https://www.taiwannews.com.tw/en/news/4280848

1. What are the reasons that you think Taiwan people learned English from elementary schools or high schools for many years, but still can’t communicate with foreigners fluently?
2. Do you agree with the policy of “Taiwan to set up agency promoting bilingual by 2030”? Why or why not?
3. Do you have any good suggestion of helping promote English from the education? Can you share with us?
4. Recall how you learned English; do you think your way/method is good/efficient? Can you share your experience?
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

Article 2: Paddling and spa pools, public showers, reopen with restrictions in Taiwan
https://www.taiwannews.com.tw/en/news/4280725

1. What kinds of sports are difficult to do during the Covid-19 pandemic? Please discuss it with your group members?
2. Do you do the exercise during the Covid-19 pandemic? What kinds of sports do you do? Is it convenience to do the exercise during that period? How do you overcome it?
3. Are you a swimming-lover? How do you think the restrictions of the article? Will it cause any inconvenience to you if you are the swimmer?
4. Since it’s alert level 2 now, there are a lot of places reopen (such like zoo, amusement park, restaurants…), will you go these places after it reopen? Why or why not?
5. Have you got the vaccine? Why or why now? If you’re vaccinated, please share your side effects or any uncomfortable feeling with your group members.
