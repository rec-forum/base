Session II

How an allowance helps us learn about money

When we are children, we must have had allowance, from which we began to learn the value of money. The goal is to learn even if make a financial mistake, you won’t pay high price.

The amount of money that parents give to their children to spend as they wish differs from family to family. Timing is another consideration. Some children get a weekly allowance. Others get a monthly allowance.

Saving helps us understand that costly goals require sacrifice: you have to cut costs and plan for the future.
Although investment is risky, it can also accumulate wealth slowly. When money is used, there is no shortage of funds available.

Questions:
1. When you get the allowance, how do you want to use it?
2. What is the idea for investment and what are the benefits of financial planning?
3. Are you satisfied with your current financial plan? If you are not satisfied, what are the good ways to do it?
