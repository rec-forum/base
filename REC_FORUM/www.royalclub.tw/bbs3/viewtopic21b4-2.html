Session 1: Can music mend a broken heart?
https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish/e ... /ep-221201
Q1. Do you remember the first time you fell in love? What is the story?
Q2. What is your theme song for falling in love? Why did you choose it?
Q3. Do you believe that music can mend a broken heart? Why or Why not?

Session II: The Big Five Personality Traits
https://tw.voicetube.com/videos/123726
Q1. What do you think about the Big Five personality traits (open, conscientious, extrovert, agreeable, neurotic) claims that we can describe ourselves with five main characteristics?
Q2. Do you recognize your own personality in some of the traits?
Q3. Do you think this theory is flawed, as we cannot describe an entire personality in five parameters?
Q4. Can you recognize that the five pirates’ personalities match the Big Five, who display opposite traits to these five character types?
