Session I. A Day in the Life of a Sustainable Minimalist
https://youtu.be/LUpyStqP_1Q

1. Have you ever chosen to change your lifestyle? Why?
2. Do you think life with less stuff is boring? Why or why not?
3. Have you ever bought something that you thought would make you happy, but didn't? Share some examples with us.
4. What are you not buying anymore?

Session II. Is Your Stuff Stopping You?
https://youtu.be/8Pb-hjqdjbY

1. Do you or friends accumulate things? What are they? Why?
2. Do experiences make us happier than possessions/ stuff? What’s your opinion?
3. Why do you like to buy things? What does buying make you feel?
4. How do you organize your life/ house/ work?

Extended reading:
The Joy of Less: https://youtu.be/iGHzqmnGH4A
