Session One: Losing Our Coral Reefs
https://blogs.ei.columbia.edu/2011/06/1 ... ral-reefs/

Discussion:
1. Have you heard of the global issue of coral bleaching before and how do you feel about it?
2. What are the benefits of having healthy coral reefs?
3. What causes the coral reefs bleaching and what are the impacts of losing them?
4. What sort of things we can do to help and protect coral reefs?
5. Have you seen any films or TV programmes on coral reefs that you would like to share?


Session Two: Is the world running out of fresh water?
http://www.bbc.com/future/story/2017041 ... resh-water

Discussion:
1. Have you ever experienced water shortage before? When was it and what was it like?
2. What sort of things consume a lot of water?
3. What would happen if there was a severe water shortage around the globe, and how can we prevent it?
4. Can you think of any ways to save water and what are the benefits of doing so?
