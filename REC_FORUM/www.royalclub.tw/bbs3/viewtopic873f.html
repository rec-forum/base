Article1:20 Steps Towards More Self-Directed Learning
https://www.opencolleges.edu.au/informe ... -learning/

1. Have you ever tried self learning ? If the answer is yes. How did you overcome the obstacles ?
2. Have you ever helped your friend learning something or fulfill your friend's goals? You might play music instruments or sports together.
3. Do you prefer face to face communicating or online learning ?
4. Learning through self-teaching do you have suggestions ?
5. People around the world being quarantined now in order to prevent the spread of the virus. How can they practice self study ?

Article2:Top 10 Ways Coronavirus Will Change Your Life Forever
https://listverse.com/2020/03/24/top-10 ... e-forever/

1. Among top 10 ways of the articles which one will change your life most ?
Will the pandemic change your life ?
2. There are 33 millions confirmed cases and 1 million people died from the pandemic. When the pandemic will end ?
Is there a way to going back to normal life in the near future ? What is our future ?
3. The pandemic affect our live, work, interact, live and even grief for losses. How do you cope with it ?
4. Poor people, homeless and people with disability are vulnerable from the pandemic.
How the society and government help them ?
5. Do you see any unexpected benefits and opportunities from the pandemic ?
