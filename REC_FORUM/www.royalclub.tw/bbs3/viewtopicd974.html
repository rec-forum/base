Session I:
7 Ways to Help Eliminate Knee and Joint Pain Naturally
http://hhdresearch.org/eliminate-knee-pain-naturally/9/
Q1. Why is it bad if we have knee problems?
Q2. Do you have knee problems? If yes, what are your symptoms? If not, how do you keep them healthy?
Q3. If you have knee problems which way from the article will you try?
Q4.Do you know any other treatment for knee pain?
Q5. What is your advice for someone with knee problems?
Session II:
How To Simplify Your Life
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7lECIsRif10

https://www.theschooloflife.com/thebook ... %20Article
Q1. Why do many of us long for a simpler life more than anything else?
Q2. What are we so stressed? Why do we feel overwhelmed by our responsibilities, schedules, commitments and obligations?
Q3. In order to sift through the chaotic contents of our minds, we can ask ourselves the following questions, “What is making me anxious? Who has caused me pain and how? What is exciting me?”
Q4. What are some of the things we may need to do to simplify our lives? Any suggestions?
Q5.Would you take steps towards a simpler life or do you plan to do so in the future?

8:15 ~ 8:45 pm presentation, sharing the article & other experiences
