Questions:
1. Traditionally we believe "people often think that they need to be married to be happy." However, this new study finding is opposite: Being married doesn't make you happier than staying single. What do you think about the result of the study? Or why single people can have a happy life?

2.The researcher said that "if you're unhappy to begin with, getting married is not likely to drastically change that.'' Do you agree with this quote and why?

3.If you are a parent, would you encourage your children to get married? Or what goes through your mind when your parents ask you to get married?

4.Taiwanese and other countries' governments have taken actions and set incentives to increase marriage rate and birth rate, do you agree with that and why?

5. If the study result gets published widely on media, what trends or thoughts will happen to younger generation?
