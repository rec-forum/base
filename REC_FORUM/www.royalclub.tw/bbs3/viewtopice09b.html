Session 1
Very high’ odds of war with China, US Republican warns
https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2023/1/2 ... s-official

Question
1. The U. S. predicts that the China will attack Taiwan in 2025. If it’s true, how will you respond? Are you willing to fight for Taiwan if necessary? why and why not?
2. Is this war really inevitable? If it happened, can you imagine what the result would be in Taiwan?
3. If you were the leader of Taiwan, how would you deal with the current problems?
4. From a historical perspective, do you see the US as a peacekeeper or warmaker, why?

3. What do you think are the pros and cons of being married or not?
4. Do you think it’s a good idea to have a trial marriage before getting married, and why?
