Hi all, I’m Zet, the host of 6/1’s meeting. I choose 2 speeches from TED and make some questions for you guys. Hope everyone interested in them. Sorry about so late to post the articles, you will realize why in session 1.

Session 1: Inside the mind of a master procrastinator

It is talking about the Instant Gratification Monkey & the Panic Monster in procrastinators’ brain.

http://tedxtaipei.com/articles/nside_th ... astinator/

Transcript : https://www.ted.com/talks/tim_urban_ins ... n#t-243708

Q1: Do you think most of time you are a procrastinator or not ? What kinds of things or situation will let you be a procrastinator ?

Q2: What are you doing with your Instant Gratification Monkey when he takes your wheel usually ? What’s your feel when you play with him ?

Q3: How early before the due date that your panic monster wake up usually ? Did he always save you? or your monkey is stronger than him?

Q4: Have you gotten unhappiness or regrets in some situations because the panic monster keep hibernation ? Are you really suffer in that or you got a lot of fun with Instant Gratification Monkey ?

Q5: Do yo think the method of the speaker mention in the end is useful to take Instant Gratification Monkey away ? or you have better ideas which can share to others?

Session 2: How to make stress your friend

The lady said the dying risk of the stressful people who don’t believe stress will harm their health is lowest than most of people. That is totally reverse the thing we usually believe.

https://www.ted.com/talks/kelly_mcgonig ... uage=zh-tw

Transcript : https://www.ted.com/talks/kelly_mcgonig ... anguage=en

Q1: Could you share your opinion about the relationship of stress & health ? Is stress good or bad for your health ?

Q2: Do you believe the result of the lady said ? Will people be healthier If they think stress is not harmful their body ?

Q3: Do you think the stress reaction is mean your body ready to take the challenge you facing ? Can you think that way when you get pressure ?

Q4: Are you surprise about that stress makes you social ? Can you feel more compassion when you got stress ? Have you had this kind of experience similar with that situation?

Q5: Would you like to pick a stressful job with meaning or easy job avoid discomfort ? Will you change your decision after you listen this speech ?

Apologize again, I can not attend this meeting because I have to deal with something in my company in the same evening. Hope you guys get lots of fun.
