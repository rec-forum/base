Host : Morris

Session I. Reasons to Remain Single
Pleas watch the following video, and come to the meeting to share your opinions:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=350qUmbcAZU

Questions:
1. Do you agree that “romantic love is a dangerous illusion” as said in the video? Why or why not?
2. Do you agree that “no one think their partner is terrific after a while” as said in the video? How long have you met your current partner? Is he/she still terrific? Why or why not?
3. Why are pros and cons of besing single? Do think being single is better than getting married? Why or why not?


**************************************************************************************************************

Session II. Pension reform

Please read the following news and come to the meeting to share your opinions,
http://www.chinapost.com.tw/taiwan/national/national-news/2017/07/01/499133/president-tsai.htm

Questions:
1. Do you think the passage of pension reform in the Legislative Yaun for civil servants, public school teachers and political appointees are sucessful? Why or why not?
2. Afer the reform, what will be the possible impacts or changes on the people reformed and the whole country?
3. What is your plan for your retirement? How can we live a better retired life?

**************************************************************************************************************
