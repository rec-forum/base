Place: 丹堤咖啡, 台北市延平南路107號2樓 (MRT 西門站4號出口)

Agenda:
6:30-7:15 pm Free talk
7:15-7:50 pm Session 1 discussion
7:50-8:15 pm Presentation (At least two members of each table are invited, 2 mins / each member)
8:15-8:45 pm Session 2 discussion
8:45-9:00 pm Presentation

Session 1: Year-end reflection

https://gavinedley.com/december-a-month-for-reflection/

Questions:
1.In 2020, did you experience any change in your life? Bad and good?
2.This year, is there anyone you are so thankful to or for what?
3.What do you think you would like to make anew or let go of this year? Family, relationship, career, time or money management, spiritual life, health ----?
4.What did you learn the most this year in our club?
5.What do you think you can do to improve your English speaking?

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Session 2: Action figure collection

http://geektoyscollectibles.com/%EF%BB% ... ollecting/

Questions:
1.Do you agree with the author’s opinion in the article about toy collection or action figure?
2.Do you collect any action figure? Why? Why not?
3.Do you know any story about collecting things, your friends? Social celebrity?
4.If you want to collect an action figure, what would you like to collect? Why?
