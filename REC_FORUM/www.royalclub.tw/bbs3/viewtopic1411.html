Session 1

Higher inflation squeezing U.S. consumers as food prices, rents accelerate

https://www.reuters.com/world/us/us-con ... 021-10-13/

1. Do you think the cost of living has increased over the last few years? Do you think the prices are still on the rise?

2. In your opinion, what consumer products are getting more expensive? What are getting cheaper?

3. Does raising minimum wage increase cost of living? What are the pros and cons of raising the minimum wage?

4. Federal Reserve Chair Jerome Powell asserted that high inflation is transitory. What do you think?

5. What are the main causes of inflation? Who benefits from it?


Session 2

China to ban kids from playing online games for more than three hours per week

https://www.cnbc.com/2021/08/30/china-t ... -week.html

1. Do you support the rules by Chinese government to cut children's online gaming time? Do you think we should have similar rules in Taiwan?

2. If you are a parent, would you limit your kids’ video game-playing time? What other limits would you give to your kids?

3. Do you agree with Chinese state media that online game is “opium” and should be restricted to prevent addiction?

4. In your opinion, what things and activities can people become addicted to? Should governments ban or restrict these addictions?

5. Do you think some people are more vulnerable to addiction than others? What are the traits of an addictive personality?
