02/23 (Thu) Trying to eat well on a budget? Here's a full week of nutritious meals that cost less than $5 a day / China’s young ‘lie flat’ under social challenges (Host: George, Meeting May)
We will hold an on-site meeting only.
摩斯漢堡明曜店(每人低消 :60元)
台北市仁愛路4段151巷37號地下室.
MRT忠孝敦化站3號出口 , 明曜百貨後方第二個巷子口


Agenda
7:00-7:30 pm ：opening ＆free talk
7:30-8:00 pm: session I, the first topic, subgroup discussion
8:00-8:30 pm: session 2, the second topic ,subgroup discussion.
8:30-9:00 table topic



--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Session I :Trying to eat well on a budget? Here's a full week of nutritious meals that cost less than $5 a day.
https://www.insider.com/guides/health/d ... budget?amp

Question
1. Are you trying to eat healthy? Do you spend a lot of money?
2. What is your monthly budget for eating?
3. Do you do the shopping list when you do grocery shopping?
4. Article says that you can decrease the spend for doing meal prep. Have you ever try this before?
5. Please share your personal tips for saving money especially on eating budget.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Session II: China’s young ‘lie flat’ under social challenges
https://www.eastasiaforum.org/2022/10/2 ... hallenges/

Question
1. What is “lie flat”? Have you ever heard this word before?
2. Do you “lie flat”? Is it ok to “lie flat”?
3. What are the possible reasons that there are many young generations who “lie flat”?
4. Is it immoral to “lie flat”, especially in the workplace?
5. Since the global economy is getting worse and worse. Do you think that the younger generations will become “lie flat”? Why?
