10 Benefits to Studying Abroad | Study Abroad Guide
https://www.internationalstudent.com/st ... ng-abroad/
Questions
1.Do you agree the benefits in the article, or is there any disadvantage of studying abroad?
2.Do you want to study abroad? Or do you regret you ever studied abroad? Or if you can restart your life, will you plan to study abroad?
3.If you want to study abroad, which country do you prefer? why?
4.Do you think studying in China is a good idea?
The second one:
15 Little White Lies Everyone Is Guilty of Telling Every Day
https://www.rd.com/advice/relationships/common-lies/
Questions:
1.Is telling white lies good or bad?
2.Have you ever told a white lie? or give examples you’ve ever heard or been told.
3.If you were doctor or the elder’s relative, would you tell the elder the truth when they’re diagnosed with cancer?
4.Will you have courage to correct club member’s mistake in speaking or writing English, or just tell a white lie (e.g. you’re great)
