Six leadership fundamentals from Michael Jordan
https://www.inc.com/bernard-coleman/six ... ordan.html

4. What do you think are the advantages or disadvantages of being a leader or a general worker?

Seven luck superstitions
https://www.thebalanceeveryday.com/luck ... gin-895272
Questions:
1. In Taiwan, do you know any interesting and meaningful superstition? share them with us.
2. The lunar calendar plays an important role in Chinese culture, what do you do when you use it? If you don’t follow it, what will happen? For this, do you really believe it or not?
3. Taiwan has a ghost month in the lunar July, it is generally taboo to do any activities, would you follow it or not? Why?
4. There are wishing pools all over the world including Taiwan, if it can really fulfill your wish, what wish would you make?
