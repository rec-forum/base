The Moon Confuses Scientists
The moon, how many years has it been following the earth? Perhaps before there were no humans on the earth, it was looking at the earth every day. In the past, everyone said that there was a Guanghan Palace in the moon, and there lived an ancient beauty - Chang'e, a white rabbit, and Wu Gang, who was cutting down osmanthus trees every day. However, on July 19, 1969, the U.S. Helios 11 spacecraft landed on the moon, but did not see Guanghan Palace, did not find Chang'e and White Rabbit, let alone Guishu and Wu Gang, so many people's beautiful fantasy became a scientific disappointment . However, it has been 26 years since astronauts landed on the moon. Humans' understanding of the moon has not increased. On the contrary, scientists have become more and more ignorant of the information obtained from the instruments left by the astronauts on the moon. Confused, whenever I look up at the moon at night, I have a complex emotion that is both familiar and unfamiliar, and I can't help but ask: the moon! Can you tell us the truth about you?

In 1970, Russian scientists Alexander Scherbakov and MihKai Vasin proposed a shocking "spaceship moon" theory to explain the origin of the moon. They believe that the moon is not actually a natural satellite of the earth, but a star that has been transformed by some kind of intelligent creature. It is excavated and transformed into a spacecraft. It contains a lot of information about the civilization. The moon was intentionally placed on the earth. In the sky, all the mysterious discoveries of the moon are the masterpieces of the higher creatures that still live in the interior of the moon.
