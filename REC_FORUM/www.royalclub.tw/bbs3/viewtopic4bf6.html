Session 1
Zara Rutherford tries to break record for
youngest pilot to fly solo around the
world
https://abcnews.go.com/International/za ... d=81743706

Question
1. A 19-year-old girl drove 51,000 kilometers alone in a light plane to travel
around 52 counties around the world. This sounds incredible, have you
ever wondered how she did it, because the whole process involves very
complicated issues, right? How do you feel about this? Do you know
what kind of challenges she has to face and deal with by herself during
this trip?
2. If your child wants to do something extremely challenging and
dangerous, if this matter is meaningful, will you agree and support or
even encourage her?
3. Have you ever achieved or engaged in extremely difficult and
challenging things or still dream of doing something crazy or pursuing
something out of the ordinary, please share with us?
4. Do you know the motivation for this young girl this time? What kind of
enlightenment or model will it bring to women or the world?
Session 2
WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT
HOMELESSNESS IN TAIWAN
https://borgenproject.org/what-you-need ... wprocket=1

Question
1. According to statistics, there were about 9,000 homeless people in
Taiwan in 2016, including more than 4,600 in new Taipei city and about
1,000 in Taipei. Do you know the reason why they become homeless?
How do they survive? Were they worthy of sympathy and why?
2. Our government want to help them provide with shelters, but most of
them are reluctant to move in. Do you really know why?
3. Homeless people sleeping on public aera seem to be affect the
appearance of the city and the image of the country, whenever you see
a group of homeless people at Taipei Main Station or an important park,
how do you feel?
4. In your opinion, is there any way to bring homeless people back to
society or the workplace and make them disappear gradually?
5. Winter is coming, have you ever thought about providing anything to
help them? Do you know what they need most and through which
agency or organization to help them?
