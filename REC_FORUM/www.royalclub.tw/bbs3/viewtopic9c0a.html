Session 1: 7 strategies for breaking a bad habit

https://au.reachout.com/articles/7-stra ... -bad-habit

Questions:
1.Do you have any bad habit? Do you want to quit/change the bad habit? What will you do for it?
2.What causes you have the bad habits? (Such like lazy cause you don’t do exercise and gain weight, pressure causes you addict to smoking/alcohol/video game, friend’s influence…)
3.Have you ever tried the tips of the articles to change/quit your bad habits? Does it work? Please share your experience.
4.Do you have any good habit that you’re proud of? What is it? How do you achieve the good habit? Can you share your experience?

Session 2: Does your school matter?

https://tw.voicetube.com/videos/29175

Questions:
1.Are you proud of your academic performances and the school where you graduated from? Why or why not?
2.Do you agree with the phenomenon is known as the Big Fish - Little Pond Effect? Do you have the similar experience at school or your office? Please share with us.
3.Expect for the school, what else do you think that it’s more important to you? (Such like your major, the future perspective, interest…)
4.If the time could come back, would you choose the same school/major/job? Why or why not? If you’d like to choose different school/major/job, what are they? Why? Please share with us.
