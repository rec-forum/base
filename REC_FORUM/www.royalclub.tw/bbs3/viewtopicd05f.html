Host : Zet

Session 1
This article is talking about microbe & mars from 60 seconds science

https://www.scientificamerican.com/podcast/episode/when-we-fly-to-mars-microbes-will-too/

Discussion
1. Have you hear about “60 seconds science” ? How do you think about this ?
2. Do you have any good tips to deal with bathroom humid problem? Or got some special experience about that. Please share with us.
3. If mars were a good place for human living, would you like to be there? What kinds of factor will force you to live there rather than living in earth?
4. Do you think mars should be more clean for human living or should be more nature and verity microorganisms like earth?
5. If you can hatch certain kind of microorganisms to separate in mars, what kind of microbe you will hatch?


Session 2
The speaker mentions that body language affects how others see us, but it may also change how we see ourselves

https://www.ted.com/talks/amy_cuddy_your_body_language_shapes_who_you_are

Discussion
1. Do you think body language is really affecting yourself when you do it? We did some of them when we grow up. Share your experience for us.
2. Do you think that you deceive yourself to be something is really helping you to achieve your goal? Did you try that before?
3. Do you have some great body language which can share with us? Or some bad experience can share?
4. Do you think Tai Chi (太極) or some Chinese kungfu are the same theory as this rule? Let psychical to influence physical?
5. If the speaker’s speech is worked on you. Do you think you can shape other people to force them to do something you want them to do?
