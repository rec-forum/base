Session I
How to deal with depression?
https://www.taiwannews.com.tw/en/news/4628832

Questions:

1. What do you think of the news?
2. Did you ever feel so depressed? What happened? How did you deal with it?
3. If you find your loved one in deep depression, how could you help him or her? Any story to share?
4. How can a couple maintain a good marriage?
5. What happens that could push a person to the edge of suicide? How to prevent it?
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Session II

‘impromptu discussion + impromptu speech’

For impromptu discussion time (40 minutes), each person prepares one or two questions for the small group discussion.
For impromptu speech time (20 minutes), the host will pick some people to answer his questions or take turns. The purpose of impromptu speech (Table topic) is to train the speaker to give a speech without any preparation.
Speaking time is about 2 minutes.
